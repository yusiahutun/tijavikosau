Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper were a couple no one seemed to have on their 2023 bingo cards, but from the latest development, it seems like it wasn’t just a fling, as she’s said to have already met his mom.

Gigi Hadid ‘meets Bradley Cooper’s mom’ after Golden Globes

Bradley Cooper attended the awards show with his mother, as they both adorably matched in black outfits. The Hangover star was nominated for two Globes on the night, for his film Maestro. Although he didn’t win an award this time, he and his cloest still went out to celebrate his incredible achievements.

Now the two look like their in with the moms, it may be more serious than once thought.
