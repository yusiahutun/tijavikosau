Bella Hadid sparkles in a thigh-split sequin dress for Bulgari dinner in Milan

May 2024 Â· 1 minute read

Bella Hadid sparkled in sequins for a Bulgari dinner in Milan last night.

The 21-year-old arrived at the party at the Bulgari Hotel wearing a glittering black Vivienne Westwood couture gown with a thigh-high split. The event was held in celebration of the brand's spring/summer 2019 leather goods and accessories collection.

Daniele Venturelli//Getty Images

The asymmetric dress featured an exposed corset and a sheer overlay covered in glittering sequins. She paired it with black ankle-strap courts, a pink diamond Bulgari necklace and Bulgari clutch bag.
