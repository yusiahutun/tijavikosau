With the look of a macho man, model-turned-actor Keith Powers has highlighted his screen romance with women. But, behind the reel, he kept his love affairs with his girlfriend quiet.

Despite a low-key personal life, Keith openly spoke about the type of woman who would be compatible enough for him. Now, let’s know about his ideal woman, followed by his sexual preference and dating status.

The Ideal Girlfriend

In an interview with Whosay, Keith revealed the stuff he had done for a girl whose name got never disclosed. He said:

“The cutest thing I’ve done is send flowers and chocolate. I’ll, like, write on the card. I’ll write a paragraph.”

Similarly, on the other hand, when asked by a source about the kind of girls he usually goes for and about his dream girls, he said:

"Girls who do not need a man. Girls who are so focused they do not even have the time to give a man the time of day because of their career. My dream girl right now is Zoe Kravitz or Zendaya. Both my crush."

He hinted that he needed a working girlfriend.

Onscreen Relationships Plus Gay Rumors

In the series Faking it, Keith Powers (Theo) was caught locking lips with his co-star Bailey De Young (Lauren).

And in the meantime, he was spotted spending a relaxing time with Saweetie Harper off the screens. However, no confirmation got received on the speculation of their dating.

Being a handsome man but with no confirmed details on relationship status, there were rumors that the actor was gay. However, that all changed when he came out public with his dating status with a girlfriend.

Dating Ryan Destiny

The woman in Keith's life is Girl Meets World's actress, Ryan Destiny. The pair met at a Teen Vogue party in 2015 and began dating shortly after.

The couple often makes appearances on each other Instagram. On Ryan's 23rd birthday (8th of January 2018), the actor posted a picture of himself and his girlfriend and wrote how he feels blessed to be with someone like her.

How Much Does Keith Powers Earn?

Keith switched from the sports to the entertainment zone and landed a role in Pretty Little Lairs' television series in 2013. After that, he worked for several other series and movies like Straight Outta Compton (2015), Faking It (2015), and Recovery Road (2015).

He is also a familiar face for brands like Calvin Klein, GQ, Guess, and Sketchers.

With all his hard work, he has a net worth of $1 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Moreover, his net worth shall be further enhanced as he has undertaken a major series project, Famous In Love (2017).
