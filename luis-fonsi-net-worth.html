Luis Fonsi Biography

Luis Alfonso Rodriguez Lopez-Cepero, who is known all around the world by his professional name Luis Fonsi, is a very successful figure in Puerto Rico. Fonsi was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico, on 15 April, 1978. He spent his early years there, with his parents, Alfonso Rodriguez, and Delia Lopez-Cepero. Fonsi has two siblings as well, both of them younger than him. One of his brothers, Jean Rodriguez is also a very famous singer.

He was quite active since his childhood, and he always dreamed of being a singer. He used to idolize the popular group Menudo. He also used to sing in San Juan Children’s choir during his childhood. Later, his family moved to Orlando, Florida, when he was 10 years old. He completed his studies there and later entered the music industry.
