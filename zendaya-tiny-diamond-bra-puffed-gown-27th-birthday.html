Zendaya Is a Birthday Vision in a Tiny Diamond Bra and Patterned Gown

March 2024 · 2 minute read

It may be Zendaya's birthday, but the star is giving us the gift of a truly jaw-dropping look.

Today the Emmy-winning actress shared glowing photos of the opulent look she wore to celebrate her 27th birthday last night. The opulent, innovate ensemble is comprised of a voluminous skirt with a pink and purple swirling pattern, and a matching long-sleeved crop top that tied at the front and entirely revealed her torso. She added the tiniest triangle string bra top, which was covered in diamonds.

The star went sans accessories, keeping the focus on her stunning outfit while posing in front of a beige stone wall. As for her glam, she wore her dark-brown bob pulled back into a ponytail, with flawless glowing makeup, shimmering eye shadow, and a light pink lip.

The Euphoria star celebrated her special day privately, though she did post thank you messages for fans' birthday wishes on social media. She paired her heartfelt Instagram post with a childhood throwback pic, where a little Zendaya smiles widely at the camera while a plate of chocolate chip cookies sits in front of her.

"Every year I grow I’m reminded of how precious this life is, thank you all for helping me fill it up with so much love. Your kind words and constant support means everything to me. Thank you so so much and here’s to 27," she wrote.

Tom Holland also joined in on the birthday fun yesterday, sharing several photos of his girlfriend to his Instagram Story, per People. In one adorable candid pic, Zendaya is seated on a boat wearing full snorkeling gear as she gives a thumbs-up to the camera. "My birthday girl," Holland captioned the pic, adding a heart-eyes emoji.
