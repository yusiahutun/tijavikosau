6 Kenyan celebrities with the most exquisite Mercedes Benz

September 2024 · 3 minute read

- Owning a modern Mercedes Benz is a clear sign that you have truly made it in the game and Kenyan celebrities know this all too well

- The 254 showbiz personalities have made their might known with some exquisite ride, most of which fall in the Mercedes category

- Here are some of the flashiest Benzes as flaunted by our very own celebrities

What is the epitome of success? Many would say a nice house, good faithful spouse and a nice ride to top it all off. Let’s focus on the third one for a minute.

Kenyan celebrities surely know how to live large, and this can be exhibited by the kinds of rides we see roaming the streets of the capital (very rarely of course)

Take a look at some of the Mercedes Benz models graced by some top personalities in Kenya:

1. Jeff Koinange - Mercedes G class Wagon

Don’t let his misfortune in KTN fool you. This guy is balling like something else. He arguably has one of the most expensive machine in the lot. His G class wagon goes for an outstanding 11 million shillings.

2. Eric Omondi and Bae - E200 compressors

His entire social media spells “Goals”. From numerous pictures with his Italian babe Chantal to the lavish lifestyle he leads. He recently gave us a tease of what he and his boo drive around when they are on separate missions. The E200 compressors, and twin ones for that matter.

3. Larry Madowo - Mercedes Benz ML Series

To Larry, nothing but a good life will do. “The Trend” host, who happens to be close pals with Jeff Koinange is also his close rival as far as costly machines are concerned. His ML 2016 series Benz in the market today will cough you a staggering 7 million shillings (10 million if you opt for a new one).

4. Chris Kirubi - Maybach Benz

The renown philanthropist and businessman should top it all off. His machine can literally buy all the Benzes in the list. Kirubi, Director at Centum investment company became the first Kenyan to own the 400,000 dollar Maybach Benz.

Just to be a less vague, Kirubi's Maybach is valued at around 43 million shillings abroad. Other personalities with the same include Kim Kardashian, Birdman (rapper), David Beckham, Nicholas Cage, and one of the richest rappers alive - P Diddy.

This young lass is making waves all over. Be it for having a stunning ‘prayer partner’ or simply producing some good gospel hits, Bahati has shown that he is more than his name by flaunting a clean blue Mercedes valued at around 2 million.
