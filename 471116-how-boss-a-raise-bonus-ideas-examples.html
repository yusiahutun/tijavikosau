Appreciation messages for a bonus

Always acknowledge your boss when you receive a bonus. Your boss does not have to give bonuses; you received one because he felt you deserved it. Even if it's an end-of-the-year holiday bonus that the entire company receives, it's still a gesture that deserves an official thank you. Here are SMS to your boss for a salary increment.

  • I'm grateful for the recent bonus, and I'm astounded by the given percentage. Happy holidays and I look forward to being a member of your team this upcoming year.
  • The bonus was an awesome surprise, and I am so grateful. I look forward to continuing to work for the company for many years; happy New Year.
  • I am grateful and delighted with this year's bonus. Recognizing my financial contributions is exciting and humbling; I look for more great things next year.
  • Your leadership has taught me a lot, and I appreciate the opportunity to work on your team. The bonus, which recognizes our achievements, is a pleasant surprise.
  • What a wonderful bonus to have this month. Since I joined your team less than six months ago, I wasn't expecting to be included. I appreciate your attention.
  • I'm appreciative that I can help the team out. You're a fantastic boss, and I love working for you: happy new year, and thanks for the bonus.
  • This year's bonus made me feel respected and appreciated is why I enjoy it. Thank you for taking the time to review and accept my suggestions for the IT project.
  • I like working with you and the other team members, and I appreciate this Christmas bonus. You are a great supervisor who has always pushed us to achieve our goals.
  • I appreciate you awarding me the bonus because working as a team was thrilling. We did great this year; perhaps next year will be much better.
  • I appreciate the recent bonus. It will be useful, but more than that, knowing that my efforts are being noticed makes me feel quite confident. I'm extremely appreciative to work for a business that lavishly expresses gratitude.
  • Thank you for the bonus! I'm hoping the business will have an even better year next year.
  • I want to thank you for the bonus this year. Being admired is always pleasant, and it benefits your finances.
  • We are extremely fortunate to have a compassionate boss. I appreciate the kind gesture of the present.
  • I'm thrilled to have gotten a bonus. I appreciate you appreciate the effort.
  • You make a fantastic mentor and supervisor. Thank you so much for this year's increment.
  • Your talents are just as admirable as your leadership and direction. Please accept our sincere appreciation for the presents.

What to say when you get a raise

You want to go above and above by sending a thank-you message to your boss after they recently gave you a raise to express how grateful you are. Your instincts are sound; however, whoever stated that stringing two simple sentences together (like "thank you") is easy must have made life by creating greeting cards. Thank your boss for a raise in person by saying the following.

  • I'm happy to be a part of your team. I appreciate the annual merit raise. More money in the bank is always welcome.
  • It feels nice to be rewarded with a raise and bonus! We are grateful for your financial support.
  • I'm appreciative of the recent pay raise! I appreciate the portion that was given to me.
  • The bonus came as a fantastic surprise. I'm also appreciative of the pay boost. I am looking forward to starting working for the company and seeing what the upcoming year holds.
