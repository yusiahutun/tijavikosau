Biography

Nivek Ogre was born in Calgary, Canada on December 5, 1962. Best known for founding and performing with the Vancouver-based electronic band Skinny Puppy, this singer and instrumentalist also co-founded a band called ohGr. Also an actor, he appeared in such horror films as Scream Park and The Devil’s Carnival. Using the pseudonym of “Hypo Luxa,” vocalist Al Jourgensen produced music for Ogre and Skinny Puppy. In the early 1980s, he and musician cEvin Key (Kevin Crompton) became co-founders of Skinny Puppy.
