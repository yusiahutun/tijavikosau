Early Life

When she was 12, she started participating in competitive roller skating, and while attending Barrington High School, she described herself as being “the biggest dork.”

In 1992, when she was 17, she appeared in the Miss Teen USA pageant, where she placed seventh overall.

After graduating, she moved to Miami to pursue a modeling career and later to New York, where she was named Miss New York USA in 1995.

The same year, she was runner-up to Chelsi Smith at the Miss USA pageant, but she inherited the title once Smith was crowned Miss Universe.

Career

The last couple of films she appeared in were in the early 2000s, including Big Momma’s House 2 in 2006 and I Love the New Millennium in 2008.
