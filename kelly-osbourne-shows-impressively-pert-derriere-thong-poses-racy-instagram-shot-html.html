She has been a poster girl for fitness and a healthy lifestyle ever since losing weight while she competed on Dancing With The Stars.

And Kelly Osbourne is certainly proud of the results if her raunchy Instagram on Friday is anything to go by.

The 29-year-old posed with her jeans around her knees displaying a bright pink thong and a very toned behind. She captioned: 'It's not a #FullMoon but it feels like one! Good luck everyone tonight!'

Full moon? Kelly displayed her impressively pert behind in this Instagram shot from Friday

Her hairstylist Ryan Randall sits next to her admiring the view and curiously clutching a bouquet of flowers.

Clad in a black tank-top, underwear and a pair of white socks, the daughter of Sharon and Ozzy Osbourne looked in fantastic shape.

The funky fashion critic was on her way to the 24hourplays event in Santa Monica and while there took a humourous photo with Alanis Morrisette, using her long black locks as a beard.

'I has so much fun dancing the night away with #Alanis @morissette at the after party for @24hourplays! This dance is called #TheBeard' she said alongside the black and white snap.

A stony faced Kelly leaves a Ray-Ban party at Mack Sennett studios in Los Angeles on Saturday

Nonconformity: The highly individualistic star has been rocking a punky lilac mohawk in recent weeks

  

The Fashion Police co-host recently debuted a distinctive lilac mohawk, courtesy of her friend and stylist Ryan.

The hairdresser regularly posts pictures of his muse modelling his latest creations and Kelly seems more than happy to oblige.

On Saturday she was seen looking rather sombre leaving a Ray-Ban party at Mack Sennett studios in Hollywood.

Rocking a multitude of plaits and safety pins in her punky lavender locks, the star walked with arms folded and head down.

Heading home: The lilac haired star carried a leather jacket out of the venue after enjoying an evening of live music

However, it seems she had a blast at the event hanging out with band The Orwells, who performed at the 'Pants-off Party'.

And although she readily pulled off her jeans on Friday, there was no pants-off for the star at the Ray-Ban event as she came unprepared, revealing on Twitter: 'Had no idea that the @theorwells were having a no pants party! Thank god I forgot to wear knickers!'

The former reality star appears to be enjoying life at the moment.

In a Tweet on Saturday the TV personality exclaimed: 'In the last 6th months I have learnt so much about myself its shocking I feel like me again like nothing is stopping me. U guys R the best!'

Hairy: The fashion critic posed with Alanis Morissette on Friday night at the 24 Hour Plays event in Santa Monica

She reportedly put herself in 'food rehab' after gaining 18 pounds following her split from fiancé Matthew Mosshart five months ago.

Since then, Kelly has been linked with skater Braydon Szafranski, but she said in an interview with Mail Online that she isn't planning on being in a serious relationship any time soon.

'I plan on being single for a very, very long time. No, I don't think it’s possible for someone to sweep me off my feet right now. I’m really enjoying my life.'
