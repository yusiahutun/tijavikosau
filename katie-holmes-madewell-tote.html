Katie Holmes' Leather Tote Bag from Madewell Nails Quiet Luxury

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Quiet luxury handbags don't necessarily have to cost a fortune. Katie Holmes' Madewell tote bag, which you can score for less than $200, is proof.

Though quiet luxury was first defined by designers like The Row, Toteme, and Savette, embracing a stealth wealth mindset is less about a specific label and more about prioritizing quality materials and elegant minimalism. As Katie Holmes' Madewell Essential Bucket Tote demonstrates, an affordable tote bag can look just as high-end when chosen and styled correctly.

Madewell The Essential Bucket Tote

Madewell The Essential Bucket Tote

Over the past few weeks, Katie Holmes has worn her chocolate brown Madewell tote with everything from sweatpants, hoodies, and Adidas Sambas to fall sweaters and boyfriend jeans. In addition to dark brown, Madewell's newest tote bag comes in black, chestnut brown, and navy suede, all of which look equally as elegant. Though it may pass for a true luxury handbag, it's available for purchase at Madewell, Amazon, and Nordstrom for $178.

Whether you're a celebrity or not, an adjustable shoulder strap, magnetic snap closure, and interior pocket make Holmes' practical tote bag ideal for everyday schlepping. It's also large enough to hold a 15-inch laptop, so it functions well as a work tote or travel bag. Crafted from sturdy yet supple leather (a Madewell specialty), it's the type of piece that only gets better with age. Reviewers describe the leather material as being "rich and luxurious," and soft but able to hold its shape. They also noted that "the wide strap makes carrying a laptop much more comfortable" and that you can wear it crossbody or shorten the strap for a shoulder bag.

Trend-spotters at Firstfinds also noted that similar Madewell handbag styles, such as the Leather Transport Tote and Essential Leather Tote, have been worn by Meghan Markle, Gal Gadot, Lea Michelle and other A-listers—only adding to the brand's appeal.

With the holiday season fast approaching, this celebrity-worn Madewell bag makes an easy gift for anyone on your list. There's no telling how long Katie Holmes' exact bag will stay in stock, so it's best to secure one now.
