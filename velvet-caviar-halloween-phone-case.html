In case you didn’t know, I’m a HUGE fan of Halloween. There’s nothing quite like transforming your home into a creepy wonderland filled with pumpkins, bats, spiders, and cobwebs. Every year, I obsess over what costumes I’ll wear, what delicious treats I’ll bake, and what new ways I can spookify my life. Fellow code orange friends on TikTok are buzzing with excitement, too, and they’ve discovered the cutest phone case collection from Velvet Caviar that allows your fave device to get in on the dressing-up action.

If you’ve taken a single scroll on the clock app before, then you know who Velvet Caviar is. They’ve been killing the tech accessory game for a long, long time, with AirPods, MacBook, iPad, and Samsung cases, as well as phone grips, charms, battery packs, and more. Literally every influencer I know has one, as they’re cute and lightweight, yet super durable. Each case, available from iPhone 11 models up to iPhone 14s’, is scratch-resistant, anti-microbial (bye-bye bacteria!), and drop-tested by eight feet, offering 360-degree protection. Plus, their products come with a one-year warranty, which gives you peace of mind.

They’ve come up with many fun patterns — with some of my current faves being their new ‘Cowboy Teddy’ print and the recently restocked ‘Puppies & Kittens’ print — and collabs over the years. But nothing can top just how adorable their Halloween collection is. As someone who is clearly obsessed with animals (as seen in my picks above), I couldn’t contain my shriek of joy when I saw their ‘Spooky Baby Animals’ iPhone Case, which has a matching AirPods Case and Grip Ring. Little creatures dressed up as Halloween classics like skeletons, vampires, and witches? Sign. Me. Up. And don’t get me started on their ‘Ghosted Clear’ and ‘Ghosted Again’ iPhone Cases, which feature not-so-spooky specters flying around. Though I am a Halloween fanatic, I recognize that some people may like a subtler approach to the holiday — and dw, Velvet Caviar has got your back. Their ‘Black Cat Clear’ iPhone Case and ‘Moonlight Moths’ iPhone Case are mystical, yet neutral enough to be used all year around.
