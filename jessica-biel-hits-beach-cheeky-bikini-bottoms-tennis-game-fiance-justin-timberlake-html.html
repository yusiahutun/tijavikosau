Jessica Biel has made a name for herself as an action heroine in movies like Blade:Trinity.

And the 30-year-old beauty has made no secret of her disciplined real-life work-out schedule to get in shape for her roles.

The results of the actress's sporty routine were on full display in Puerto Rico yesterday, as she hit the beach with fiance Justin Timberlake for a game of smashball.

Having a ball: Justin Timberlake and fiance Jessica Biel hit the beach in Peurto Rico yesterday

Smashing: Jessica Biel has admitted keeping her strict workout routine is not always easy, but the results were on full display yesterday

The 7th Heaven actress, who will be seen next month in the much anticipated remake of Total Recall, wore tiny black bikini bottoms for her beach outing.

Jessica paired the bikini with a long-sleeved purple-and-white surf rash vest and black sun visor.

Justin, 31, was bare chested in a pair of black-and-blue board shorts.

The lovebirds, who became engaged in December last year, hit a smash ball between them with apparent ease.

Jessica, a former gymnast, has said she works had to maintain her killer physique.

Hiking, sprinting on a treadmill, yoga, swimming and working with a personal trainer are all in her regular routine.

'My trainer, [Jason] Walsh works with athletes, so we do a lot of football drills, like pulling big sleds and waving and waving heavy ropes,' says Biel in the August issue of InStyle magazine.

'It's intense cardiovascular training but really fun.'

The actress says she doesn't miss her training sessions even when she her work days are very long.

'I might work out as late as 9 or 10pm.,' she says.

'When you have to do it, you just do it. It's not easy at all. I just grumble through the whole way.

Beach bodies: Justin went bare chested in a pair of board shorts, while Jessica showed off her exceptional behind in a tiny pair of black bikini bottoms

The Illusionist actress has two big movies out in coming weeks.

First up will be Total Recall, the story of factory worker Douglas Quaid (Colin Farrell) who begins to suspect that he is a spy.

The movie, a remake of the original Arnold Schwarzenegger hit released in 1990, also stars Breaking Bad's Brian Cranston and British actress Kate Beckinsale.

Biel's other big Hollywood movie, slated for release on September 5, is The Tall Man, a mystery thrilled in which she plays a mother whose child goes missing.

Love match: Justin, 31, and Jessica, 30, announced their engagement in December last year

Down time: The high-profile couple have equally busy schedules

Singer, actor and entrepreneur Timberlake, meanwhile, last week got to add a new title to his resume.

The PGA of America announced the 31-year-old will serve as the first U.S. team ambassador.

Timberlake will make his debut at the 39th annual Ryder Cup in September in Illinois. 

Star power: Jessica and Justin on the red carpet earlier this year at The Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City

The Friends with Benefits star took time out from his holiday yesterday to send a message of condolence out to the victims of the Colorado shooting.

'So saddened to hear of the tragic theatre shooting last night in Aurora, Colorado,' he posted on his Twitter account.

'Sending my prayers to the victims and their families'
