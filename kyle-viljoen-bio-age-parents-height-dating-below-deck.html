Kyle Viljoen is the new cast member of Below Deck Mediterranean. The season just premiered and his action has already been scrutinized. Learn below, what exactly happened.

Meet Kyle Viljoen, Stew On Below Deck Mediterranean

Kyle Viljoen is one of the new faces in Below Deck Mediterranean season 7 which premiered on 11 July. Kyle is a second stew who is starring alongside other new casts Jason Gaskell (Deckhand), Storm Smith (Deckhand), Raygan Tyler (Bosun), Natalya Scudder (Third Stew), Dave White (Chef), and Natasha Webb (Chief Stew).

The returning members are Mzi “Zee” Dempers and Captain Sandy. The show revolves are a similar theme to the first six seasons that came before i.e. what it is like to work on a yacht and drama that sprouts when the crew members start working together.

Kyle already did something that is considered taboo in the world of yachting. He potentially kissed a guest, according to Captain Sandy Yawn, a thing that should never happen. “Let’s not forget seasons back when that happened with another crew member. You just never want to cross that line. In the corporate world, it doesn’t happen,” Sandy, 57, exclusively told Us Weekly.

Captain Sandy added that what they do once they get off the vessel is their personal life is their personal decision.

“So, if they want to hook up with clients after they’re signed off the vessel, go for it,” she added. “I’m all about love. I found love. I want everybody to have love, but you can’t do it in a professional setting”.

Kyle Viljoen Net Worth

Kyle Vilojen sits on a net worth that roughly measures under $250 thousand.

According to his Facebook, Kyle graduated from Springs Boys High School in 2010. Then, he attended the University of Pretoria where he earned his bachelor’s degree in education. He studied Cabin Crew at EPT Aviation Training and completed his training in 2015. he studied to become a facilitator at Execoach.

Kyle completed his Steward / Deckhand training at PYT – Professional Yachtmaster Training in 2019. Moreover, he studied, Ofqual-regulated Level 5 Teaching English as a Foreign Language Master Training Course at The TEFL Academy which he completed in 2020.

Starting his career in 2009, Kyle joined ATKV-Drakensville as a waiter/childcarer/entertainer. He worked as a model at Aardvark Casting Agency Gauteng from 2012 to 2014. Around the same time, he also worked at Blackberry as Brand Ambassador.

In 2014, he also delved into bartending working for Springbok Bar, Hatfield.

Kyle has worked as a flight attendant and Cabin Services Manager at Skywise Airline, FlySafair, Qatar Airways – Doha International Airport, and Africa Charter Airline. In addition, he also worked as a Guest Lounge Administrator at Fireblade Aviation. He delved into a career as a steward after 2019.

First, Kyle joined Superyachts as chief steward, worked as Temp Sole Steward at Super Yachts, then as 2nd Steward at Super Yachts, and part-time Recruitment Consultant at Indie-Pearl Yacht Recruitment.

In July 2022, Kyle is working as Freelance Chief Steward at STEWARD and ESL Teacher (English as a Second Language Teacher) at The TEFL Academy.
