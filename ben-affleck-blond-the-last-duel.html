Ben Affleck Dyes His Hair Blonde for New Film Role

May 2024 · 2 minute read
  • Ben Affleck was recently photographed with a completely new look: a full head of blond hair.
  • The Batman actor was on set for his new movie, The Last Duel, in which he plays a 14th century medieval Frenchman, which explains the new 'do.
  • The film, which he co-wrote, will also star other notable actors like Jodie Comer, Matt Damon, and Adam Driver.

A small list of things that never should've existed: Low-rise pants, five-toe running shoes, the cotton candy burrito, Michael Bloomberg's presidential candidacy, this tweet, and–now–Ben Affleck's shiny nest of platinum blond hair.

Is it a mid-life crisis? A trick of the light? Don't worry, folks, it's just a mild hair transformation he had to undergo for his new film, The Last Duel. The flick will feature performances by other notable actors, including Jodie Comer, Adam Driver, and Matt Damon. Affleck will be playing a 14th century medieval Frenchman, which is, like, really impressive because who knew that these guys had access to L'Oréal hair dyes back in the 1300s?

BACKGRID

Life as an actor is never a lonely one. In this case, Damon and Driver also got some, um, questionable makeovers.

The movie, which will be directed by Ridley Scott, is based on Eric Jager’s book, The Last Duel: A True Story of Crime, Scandal, and Trial by Combat, according to ET. Affleck and Damon also co-wrote the screenplay, marking the first time they've written together since their 1997 Oscar-winning film Good Will Hunting.

You can catch more of Affleck's blond 'do in all its silver screen glory on Christmas day, when the movie hits theaters.

So, Ben, tell us. Do blonds really have more fun?
