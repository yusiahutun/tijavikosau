Wentworth Miller: Career and Net Worth

His net worth is $4 million as of 2019. Miller commenced his career with the debut in 1998 in an American television series Buffy the Vampire Slayer. He contributed his acting talents for 20 episodes in season 2 of the show.

After a year he became part of an American drama series Time of Your Life. He featured in the television series Popular and ER in 2000 also secured a lead role in Dinotopia in 2002.

2005 became a great year for the actor as he got a starring role in an American television serial drama Prison Break portraying Michael Scofield. The role was groundbreaking for him which brought him to superstardom.

Miller also appeared in the TV shows like Ghost Whisperer and Joan of Arcadia. Wentworth has also showcased his talent through prominent roles on Law & Order: Special Victims Unit (2009), Young Justice: Invasion (2013), The Flash (2014) and Legends of Tomorrow (2016).

Wentworth Miller also appeared on the big screen with movies like The Human Stain (2003), Resident Evil: Afterlife (2010), The Loft (2014) and The Disappointments Room (2015).
