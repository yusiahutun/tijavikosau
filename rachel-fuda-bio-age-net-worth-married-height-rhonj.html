Meet RHONJ's Rachel Fuda! Her Bio, Age, Net Worth, Married, Height

March 2024 · 5 minute read

Season 12 of Real Housewives of New Jersey only completed its airing in 2022, and we had already got the news that the filming for the next season has already begun. Also, as per rumors, the brand new season was expected to bring not one, not two, but three new housewives. Although there had been no confirmation from the side of Bravo, one housewife that could be joining the show was Rachel Fuda, Melissa Gorga’s friend.

Let’s talk about Rachel’s age, job, married life, and family in the article below.

Meet Rachel Fuda, Melissa Gorga’s Friend & Rumored RHONJ Cast Member

On Monday’s episode of “The Morning Toast, Frank Catania, RHONJ’s cast member Dolores Catania’s ex-husband shared a lot of details about the upcoming season of the hit Bravo show.

In the same interview where he confirmed the rumors of Jackie Goldschneider being demoted to a “friend of” in the coming season of RHONJ, he said, “we have some new girls coming on this year.”

Fan account “rhonjobsessed” also claimed that the Season 13 cast will include new housewife Rachel Fuda in an Instagram post on May 2, 2022.

Although Bravo has not confirmed the circulating rumors yet, the fact that Rachel is from Franklin Lakes, New Jersey makes her a perfect potential housewife in RHONJ. Her official role in RHONJ has not been determined yet and will mostly become clear as filming moves on.

Not only, Rachel Fuda, but there are also two more women expected to make an appearance in the new season of The Real Housewives of New Jersey. They are Danielle Cabral (Teresa’s friend) and Jennifer Fessler (Margaret’s friend). Danielle runs a kids/teens clothing company whereas Jennifer is the CEO of a designer shoe company and lives in Upper Saddle River, New Jersey.

Rachel Fuda Age

According to some sources, Rachel Fuda’s age was 31 as of May 2022.

Rachel Fuda Job

Rachel Fuda is an entrepreneur and co-owned a restaurant, Burger Boss, with her husband John Fuda, which was located at 525 Moonachie Ave, Wood-Ridge, New Jersey. According to boozyburbs.com, the restaurant was named “Burger Boss” because the owners, Rachel and John both have Italian heritage, and they both loved movies about the mob.

Burger Boss’ beef burgers are named after famous mafia movies and characters, namely The Godfather, The Goombah, The Capo, to name a few. Besides beef burgers, Burger Boss also serves veggie burgers, hot dogs, and fries.

Before Burger Boss, 525 Moonachie Ave housed three other restaurants, namely Red Hen Bistro, Mariposa, and Black Rebel Burger. All these restaurants closed for a lot of reasons, however, one problem they all faced was a lack of parking lot.

Furthermore, according to data-lead.com, Rachel was also working at Valet King as Office Operations Manager in May 2022.

While we don’t know how much money she makes working as Office Operations Manager, the average salary for that job is around $68,500 per year in New Jersey. We guess her earnings are also somewhere around that figure.

Is Rachel Fuda Married?

Rachel Fuda is married to her husband, John Fuda, since 2017. John is the owner of Valet King since 2013, according to his LinkedIn. Valet King provides “valet parking, event parking management, and parking/traffic control services in New Jersey, New York City, Long Island, and Florida.”

Rachel’s husband has also been working at Fuda Tile and Marble as manager since 2006. John completed his high school studies at Mahwah High School.

The adorable couple celebrated their 4th wedding anniversary on January 14, 2021. Rachel took to Instagram to share a picture of the two from their wedding and wrote, “Happy Anniversary my love. Not a single dull moment in the last 4 years and I wouldn’t have it any other way. We’re so lucky to have each other. I love doing life with you  I love you Hunny!,” in the caption.

Anyone who’s ever visited either Rachel or John’s Instagram can tell how much love the two have for each other. Their socials are almost flooded with pictures of one another.

The couple is parents to three kids, two daughters and a son. Their names are Jaiden, Gianella, and Giuliana. Jaiden and Gianella were 15 and 1 years old as of May 2022. The newest member in their family, Giuliana Rose, was born on March 3, 2022. Rachel shared a picture of the newborn baby on Instagram on March 4, 2022.

Rachel Fuda Height

Rumored RHONJ star Rachel Fuda stands at an average height of 5 feet 8 inches.

Rachel Fuda Family

Talking about Rachel Fuda’s family, her parents are Artie Del Vescovo (father) and Lisa Del Vescovo (mother).

According to Lisa’s LinkedIn handle, she started at The BSG Group as District Manager in January 2014. Previously, she worked at Regis Corporation as Area Supervisor Multi Unit. Rachel’s father on the other hand worked at N.J. Transit Rail Operations as Locomotive Engineer.

Rachel wished her dad a happy birthday on Instagram on February 10, 2015. Sharing an old picture of her father holding her, she wrote, “always have been & always will be a daddy’s girl  happy birthday daddy, I love you!’ She also made an Instagram post on May 8, 2022. She then thanked her mom for everything on the occasion of Mother’s Day.

Aside from her parents in the family, Rachel also has a brother, Eric Del Vescovo, who recently got married to his wife, Jessica Domke. The two exchanged wedding vows on October 14, 2021. Eric and Jessica got engaged in 2020.
