April 2024 · 4 minute read

Kim Kardashian is no stranger to luxury, enjoying everything from her family’s property empire to a designer wardrobe fit for a king – or a reality TV queen with a US$1.8 billion dollar net worth, in her case. So it’s no surprise that her car collection is also an impressive feat with some jaw-dropping price tags to match.

Her fleet is worth US$3.8 million and includes a Lamborghini, a Rolls-Royce and a Maybach, all painted grey, reported Vanity Fair. Why the specific colour palette? To match Hidden Hills, her US$60 million California home, of course.

In a recent YouTube video with Vogue, Kardashian gave an updated tour of her home and showed off the highlights of her swanky car collection. “I’ve always been a car girl. I love cars and I really wanted something different. I wanted my cars to blend in with my house which is grey,” she told viewers. “So, I did all grey cars instead of my typical matt silver, which I used to do.”

The star paid a whopping US$100,000 on the paint jobs, according to TMZ. Here’s the best of Kardashian’s expensive vehicles.

Kim Kardashian has five Maybachs – worth a total US$1 million

For Christmas 2020, her ex-husband Kanye “Ye” West bought her five Maybachs, according to TMZ. It’s unclear what editions the rapper gifted her, but Kardashian called the Maybach Sedan her “favourite baby ever, ever, ever” in her February chat with Vogue.

The socialite’s Mercedes-Benz Maybach S580 includes an exterior repainted from factory black, with a monochrome trim design to match. “I love everything about it. I love painting the emblem grey. If I were to pick one, that’s my baby. Right there, that’s my baby,” she added about the car, which was customised by the Platinum Group.

… And a Lamborghini Urus worth US$600,000, one of her “three faves”

During the Vogue interview, Kardashian explained that her Lamborghini Urus is one of her “three faves”. It’s the first SUV from the Italian carmaker, and with four children to drive around it’s no surprise the star gets good use out of it.

Always one to opt for a unique look, she showed off the “kit on the front of it, so it looks a little different”. She gifted herself the customised Lambo with a Mansory wide-body kit and one-off wheels. The car made its debut when she stepped out of the US$600,000 vehicle in a sports bra in 2021.

However, it’s not as eye-catching as a previous stunt Kardashian pulled with her Lambo.

She has a frosty US$200,000 Lambo wrapped in Skims cosy fabric

Kardashian once wrapped her US$200,000 Lamborghini in Skims cosy fabric in the summer of 2021. “KIMMY HAD A LITTLE LAMB-BO!!! OMG isn’t this the cutest and funniest thing ever???” she wrote in an Instagram post. She added in a video: “My new ride is here … I am actually dying. It is so cute.”

According to Bustle, Paris Hilton wrote on the post, complete with emojis: “You know you’re rich rich when you can ruin a very expensive car for something like this.”

Kardashian put a “unique” spin on the Rolls-Royce Ghost for US$400,000

Kardashian has been a loyal customer of Platinum Motorsports for “a while now”, a source told Vanity Fair, adding that she has been very involved in every step of the process in creating her cars and that she “knew exactly what she wanted”.

She gushed in her Vogue video about how her latest one displays a unique frosted crystal ornament on the front instead of the regular silver version on other cars, according to Life & Style. “My Rolls-Royce is grey also. It’s a Ghost. It has the cutest crystal lady, I just think that’s so unique,” she said.
