Michelle Obama reveals very surprising items she took from the White House

March 2024 · 3 minute read

The former First Lady chose a number of select momentos

    Michelle Obama and husband Barack called the White House their home for eight years, and following their exit from the famous residence, the former First Lady decided to take a few choice items with her.

    This week, Michelle made a special appearance on , during which she shared some of the goodies she had kept from 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue.
