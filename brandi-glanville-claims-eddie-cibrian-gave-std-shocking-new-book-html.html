She has already published a best-selling book but Brandi Glanville still has plenty to get off her chest.

The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills star is making shocking new claims in a sequel to her memoir.

Brandi alleges that she caught a sexually transmitted disease from ex-husband Eddie Cibrian, who met current wife LeAnn Rimes while still married to Brandi.

She's got plenty to say: Brandi Glanville is releasing a shocking new book, Drinking And Dating: P.S. Social Media Is Ruining Romance

‘I have three permanent reminders of my marriage that I live with every day,’ Brandi writes in Drinking And Dating: P.S. Social Media Is Ruining Romance. ‘[My sons] Mason, Jake, and HPV.’

The 41-year-old blames herself for being ‘foolish enough to believe [Eddie] was faithful,’ but adds that ‘half of all sexually active Americans have HPV.’

While it seems far more recent to her, Brandi writes: ‘It’s been five years now since I discovered my ex-husband was sleeping with half of the women in Hollywood.’

Shocking claims: The reality star alleges ex-husband Eddie Cibrian, pictured with wife LeAnn Rimes last month, gave her sexually transmitted disease HPV

Lashing out: The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills star, pictured in October, writes that she was 'foolish' to believe Eddie was faithful

She adds: ‘Sometimes it feels like just yesterday that my ex and my baby boys were snuggling on the couch with me in our gorgeous Calabasas home. Other times, it feels like it was all a crazy dream.’

As usual, it is LeAnn who most of Brandi’s anger is directed at, and the reality star even brands the singer with the c-word.

Brandi describes one of her ‘favorite lessons learned’ as ‘When you write an entire c***ry album about your affair with a married man, most people won’t buy it. #JustSayin.’

Broken family: Brandi and Eddie, who were still married when he started seeing LeAnn, are parents to sons Mason and Jake. Brandi is pictured with the boys in September

Harsh words: The 41-year-old directs most of her anger at LeAnn, who she is seen with in 2012, and even calls her the c-word

Brandi, who also promises to tell all about her ‘husband’s torrid affairs with cocktail waitresses’, poses in a tight pink dress on the yellow cover while clutching a high heel.

Brandi married Young And The Restless star Eddie in May 2001 but he started an affair with LeAnn after meeting her on set of Northern Lights in 2009.

At the time LeAnn was married to Dean Sheremet, but both marriages broke down with their divorces finalised in 2010 and Eddie and LeAnn consequently married in April 2011.

Drinking And Dating: P.S. Social Media Is Ruining Romance will be released next week.
