Scholes' netball daughter, Shearer's pop star kid and what other footballers' children do

March 2024 · 4 minute read

Paul Scholes, Alan Shearer and David Beckham will always be remembered by their talents on the pitch.

But it might not be long before their children outshine their footballing dads with their own skills.

Scholes’ daughter is a netball superstar while Shearer’s is embarking on a music career having released a single.

The Beckham family are one of the most famous in the world, but son Brooklyn is working hard to forge his own career as a photographer.

While the likes of Gary Lineker and Ryan Giggs also have their own offspring looking to become successful.

And Daily Star Sport has looked at some jobs that footballers’ children do…

Paul Scholes

Alicia Scholes, daughter of Manchester United legend Paul Scholes, was named in Manchester Thunder’s 2020 Netball Superleague squad in January.

She has enjoyed a dramatic rise through England’s age groups since her first international tournament at the start of 2018 representing the country from Under-17 through to Under-21.

"We knew from aged six or seven when she first started playing at the ball hall in Failsworth," former midfielder Paul previously said. "We knew she had ability then, that she was talented and she's still as dedicated now."

Danny Mills

Former Leeds ace Danny Mills is another who has seen his child step into the competitive world with son George Mills a British champion athlete.

George won the British Indoor Championships gold medal over 1500m earlier this year having previously represented England at the Commonwealth Youth Games

Still just in his early twenties, could George be one of Team GB’s big successes in future Olympic games?

Gary Lineker

Match of the Day host Lineker has four sons, all certainly recognisable as the offspring of the 1986 World Cup golden boot winner.

Each have grown a large social media following though son Tobias has also gone down the route of becoming a DJ.

A regular at venues around London, Tobias is certainly making a name for himself on the music scene.

Alan Shearer

Alan Shearer’s daughter Hollie has sights set on becoming a pop star after releasing her track Love You Better to stream and download.

The release has already gained backing from the likes of fellow Geordies Ant and Dec, Scarlett Moffatt and her ex-England and Newcastle ace dad.

Former England captain Shearer wrote on Twitter: “Well Done Hol.”

David Beckham

Perhaps the most famous footballing dad of the lot, Beckham has four children with wife Victoria.

All famous due to their dad, eldest son Brooklyn is forging out a career as a photographer.

Brooklyn photographed a campaign for Burberry while also not afraid to get in front of the camera either as a model.

Ryan Giggs

There are plenty of players who have followed in their father’s footsteps to become a professional footballer.

And Ryan Giggs’ son Zach could be next as he trained with Manchester United last year at the age of just 12.

Ryan, who left Old Trafford when Jose Mourinho took charge in 2016, had said: "I’m actually there every Tuesday and Thursday because my son trains there. I am there quite a bit at night.”
