In the song "Crack" by 2 Chainz, the overall theme centers around the rapper's rise from humble beginnings in the trap to his success in the music industry. This line discusses the artist's past experience selling drugs on the streets, highlighting the grittiness of his background. The song ultimately conveys the message that no matter the challenges faced along the way, it is possible to rise above circumstances and achieve greatness.
