Inside Josh Hall and Christina Haacks Inspiring Love story

April 2024 · 5 minute read

Josh Hall and Christina Haack have come a long way since news of their relationship first broke. Today, the couple is happily married and determined to spend forever together.

Amid her post-divorce drama and custody battle, Christina Haack has managed to check some boxes on her dream list. Perhaps the most headline-worthy has been her marriage to new husband Josh Hall, barely one year after divorcing Ant Anstead.

In fact, Josh Hall and Christina Haack have upheld their love these past months, so much that it’s become indisputable.

From Haack’s fierce clap-back at trolls who dared to criticize her new romance, to Hall’s continuous defence of his wife on social media amid her heated court proceedings, the couple’s relationship has caught the eyes of the public in recent times.

More recently, the real estate agent and former police officer took to Instagram to praise his wife for her resilience amid her ongoing struggles. In another post, Hall voiced his determination to stand by his wife and her kids no matter what the world threw at them.

Given Hall’s dedication to his new wife, it seems Haack finally found the one after two failed marriages. In honor of their first anniversary as an official couple, here is a look into Josh Hall and Christina Haack Hall’s beautiful love story.

How Long Has Christina Haack And Josh Hall Been Together?

Josh Hall and Christina Haack have been together for a little over a year. While the exact date their relationship kicked off is unclear, the “Christina On the Coast” star first went public with their romance in a lengthy Instagram post on July 8, 2021.

The post featured Haack and her new beau cozying up to each other. Via caption, the star recounted details of their relationship and its progress so far. She reflected:

“When we met this past spring, the synchronicities hit us so hard and fast they were impossible to ignore. I felt immediately crazy protective over him and wanted to keep him for myself.”

Going further, she explained how they spent the last few months trying to know each other better before the media attention stirred in their direction. Their months of blissful privacy ended after the paparazzi busted them at the airport, forcing the couple to go public.

Notwithstanding, Haack is determined to brave the media attention, and do what she wanted with her life, irrespective of others’ opinions. And that includes falling in love again and walking down the aisle.

Christina Hack And Josh Hall’s First Meeting

You might be wondering, how did Josh Hall and Christina Haack meet? Or when they met? Interestingly, their first meeting was as uneventful as it gets.

The two met for the first time in 2010, during a real estate conference. At the time, the TV personality was still with ex-husband Tarek El Moussa.

Following their casual meeting in 2010, the two continued with their respective lives but reunited over a decade later. A source revealed the twosome reconnected in the spring of 2021, in Tennessee, following her divorce from Anstead.

Despite dealing with her divorce, and subsequent breakups from at least two more partners, Haack maintains she was in a good place emotionally when they met. She shared:

“I met Josh when I wasn’t in a state of fear or fight-or-flight.”

They kicked off their romance thereafter, and have never regretted the decision, or moments spent together.

Making Their Romance Public

Christina Haack made their relationship Instagram official in July 2021, barely a day after news outlets confirmed a budding romance between her and a mystery man. The star shared a post, detailing their love journey together. She went on to fire back at trolls who criticized her for moving on “too quickly,” making it clear their opinion hardly mattered.

She shared a post showing her and Hall seated side by side at a dinner table, with champagne glasses in hand as they commemorated her 38th birthday.

The 38-year-old spent the days that followed unapologetically sharing photos of them from her surprise birthday vacation. That same month, she gave Hall a shout-out for standing by her amid all the drama.

Working Together

In March 2022, Christina Haack announced that she and her partner established their own production company, Unbroken Productions. The venture was a fulfillment of Haack’s longtime dream of owning a company that enabled her produce anything at will.

What made the move more fulfilling was getting to share it with her husband. She admitted how much she loved having the most hard-working and creative partner in Hall.

Their Secret Marriage

Hack announced her engagement to Hall in September 2021, via a coded Instagram post comprising a set of emojis, including one of a wedding band.

The post featured a string of pictures, showing the couple posing together, with a romantic dinner set up in view. She also flaunted her engagement band in the picture, attracting bouts of congratulatory messages from fans and fellow celebrities.

In April 2022, news broke that Christina Haack tied the knot with Josh Hall. Although the mother of three never addressed these reports nor shared pictures from her said marriage, changing her name to Christina Hall was enough confirmation.

A Slice Of Paradise

That same month, the star announced her family’s move to a new home in Newport Beach from Dana Point.

In June 2022, Haack shared glimpses into their dream abode on Instagram, confirming the move into her longtime family home in Newport Beach.

Seeing how well their relationship has progressed so far, one can’t be happier for the couple, who have each braved their fair share of challenges in the last decade. 

Although Christina was far from seeing the last of her daunting days as her custody battle with ex-husband Anstead over their 2-year-old son Hudson continues to loom, having a reliable husband by her side to support and defend her makes the journey ahead more bearable.
