1. West Garfield Park

West Garfield Park is on the West Side of Chicago, Illinois. In 2014, in West Garfield Park, 21 murders occurred at a rate of 116 per 100,000, giving this neighborhood the highest murder rate in Chicago.

Today, the crime rate in West Garfield Park is about 25.7% higher than in the rest of the city. West Garfield's violent crime can be attributed to extensive gang activity in the area. The total annual crime rate of West Garfield Park is 11,126 (estimate) per 100,000 people, making it one of the most dangerous neighborhoods in Chicago.

2. North Lawndale

Originally just called Lawndale, the North Lawndale neighborhood was named by a real estate developer in the late 1800s. Its name was meant to convey a suburban feel, distant from the noisy city. However, it became busy with the flurry of nearby industry.

North Lawndale's population has declined because of the side effects of disinvestment, such as crime and poverty.

3. East Garfield Park

East Garfield Park is a high-crime neighborhood in the West of Chicago, Illinois. The total annual crime rate of East Garfield Park is 8,510 (estimate) per 100,000 people.

8. South Shore

South Shore is located on Chicago's south side and is famed for its location along the city's southern lakefront. Although the neighborhood has seen a greater than 40% decrease in residents, it's still one of the most densely populated neighborhoods on the South Side.

The community benefits from its proximity to major institutions and attractions such as the Museum of Science and Industry, the University of Chicago, accessibility to Lake Shore Drive, and more. The total annual crime rate at South Shore is 6,246 (estimate) per 100,000 people.

9. Riverdale

Riverdale, located in Illinois's far south side, developed as a farming community at the river crossing. The industries that followed, including lumber yards, distilleries, and sugar refineries, attracted many to Riverdale.

Today, many people believe the neighborhood is bad news because of gun violence. Riverdale's total annual crime rate is 5,966 (estimate) per 100,000 people.
