The Best Vanilla Essential Oils You Can Buy in 2021

March 2024 · 4 minute read

Vanilla essential oils are beloved for their delicious, homey scent. Just a whiff conjures thoughts of cupcakes, cookies, ice cream, and other vanilla-infused treats that can make your mouth water. But what, exactly, makes one oil different from another? And what should you look for when choosing one?

We did some investigating and found three top-quality vanilla essential oils that are great for adding to a carrier oil or moisturizer and softening your skin, putting in an aromatherapy diffuser to make your home smell amazing, or dropping into your next bath to make it extra luxurious.

Why use vanilla essential oils?

There are benefits to using this popular oil other than just making your home (and yourself!) smell like dessert. Aromatherapy experts say it can boost your libido, help reduce anxiety, help wounds heal faster, ease digestive upset, and even help regulate your menstrual cycles.

Adding a few drops of this sweet-smelling oil to lotions, bath oils, or to a diffuser is a great way to get all the benefits — and to enjoy its warm, comforting scent as well!

How are vanilla essential oils made?

Unlike some other essential oils, which are distilled directly from the source, vanilla essential oil is not, strictly speaking, an essential oil at all. That’s because the chemical makeup of vanilla beans does not lend itself to the distillation process in the same way that grapefruit, jasmine, echinacea, or many other fruits and plants do. For this reason, some aromatherapy aficionados are skeptical of many products that are labeled “vanilla essential oil.”

Most vanilla essential oil products are made by mixing pure vanilla extract with a carrier oil, such as jojoba. The result is not noticeably different from any other essential oil you might use — but it is important to find a vanilla essential oil that is made from the highest quality ingredients possible.
