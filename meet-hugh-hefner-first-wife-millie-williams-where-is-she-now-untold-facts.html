Meet Hugh Hefner First Wife Millie Williams. Where Is She Now? Untold Facts

July 2024 · 6 minute read

Millie Williams, born Mildred Williams, is the ex-wife of American magazine publisher Hugh Hefner. She is the existing wife of the founder of Playboy magazine. She has also starred in the documentary 60 Minutes, based on her ex-spouse’s life.

Unfortunately, Millie divorced Hugh several years ago. But what is her life like now? Has she remarried after divorcing her first husband? Does she have any children from the marriage?

In this article, we will discuss some interesting facts about Millie Williams career, net worth, and much more. Let’s start.

Millie Williams Bio, Early Life, Education

The beautiful Millie Williams was born Mildred “Millie” Williams on March 10, 1926, in Chicago, Illinois, USA. She reportedly had a formidable childhood in which she lived with her parents. Unfortunately, she has not revealed much about either her father or her mother.

As far as her education is concerned, she went to a local school in her hometown. Later, she attended Northwestern University, where she studied sociology, but dropped out after the first semester.

She Has Appeared In Documentaries

Millie Williams is not a professional actress but has appeared in several documentaries. In 1993, she took part in the documentary series “60 Minutes”. The series, based on the life of her husband and Playboy magazine founder Hugh Hefner, depicted their life together.

Later, she also starred in another documentary, E! True Hollywood Story in 2006.

Millie Williams Is The First Wife of Hugh Hefner

Millie Williams married her college sweetheart Hugh Hefner on 25 June 1949. Both were 23 years old at the time of the wedding. They were married for about 10 years until they divorced on 18 March 1959.

But when and how did the former couple first meet? Well, they first met when they were students at Northwestern University. While they were still students, they became extremely close and started dating.

Their romantic relationship soon developed into a marriage.

She Had Two Children With Hefner

Hugh Hefner’s first wife, Millie Williams is the mother of two children. Both of her children were born to the late Playboy founder Hefner.

Her first child, a daughter named Christie Hefner, was born on 8 November 1952. Three years later, on 30 August 1955, her second child, a son, David Hefner, was born.

All her children are well-settled and successful in their professions. In fact, Christie manages her family business very well. As reported, from 1988 to 2008, she was the President of Playboy Enterprises, Inc. CEO and Chairman.

Why Did Millie Williams Divorce Hefner?

As reported, Millie Williams and Hugh’s marriage broke down due to their infidelity. This was at a time when the couple was not yet married and Hefner was serving in the army when Millie was involved in an affair.

Guilt over her infidelity led her to allow Hefner to continue his extramarital affair after the marriage. The Hollywood Reporter covered the story years later when Millie admitted her mistake,

Hef’s first real heartbreak came prior to tying the knot, when Williams admitted to engaging in an affair during his time in the Army. In an attempt to lessen her guilt and save their relationship, Williams allowed the future mogul to become involved with other women during their marriage.

Later, Hefner also commented on how devastating the news of his wife’s affair had been. He declared that he had abstained from sexual relations with other women and saved himself for his wife. He said,

I had literally saved myself for my wife, but after we had sex she told me that she’d had an affair. That was the most devastating moment in my life. My wife was more sexually experienced than I was. After that, I always felt in a sense that the other guy was in bed with us, too.

Mildred Williams Married Again After Divorce With Her First Husband

After her divorce from Hugh in 1959, Millie remained single for quite a long time. Eventually, she walked down the aisle a second time with a guy called Edwin Gunn.

He even adopted her children after their marriage and they changed their surname to Gunn. But unfortunately, their relationship didn’t last long this time either. A few years after their marriage, the couple divorced and the children changed their surname back to Hefner.

Hugh Had Two More Kids From Other Marriages

Millie’s ex-partner Hugh remarried twice after divorcing Millie. Almost 30 years after his divorce from his first wife, he married for the second time American model and actress Kimberly Conrad. The two got engaged in 1989, despite a 36-year age difference.

They had two children, Cooper Hefner and Marston Hefner. Cooper was born on 4 September 1991 and Marston on 9 April 1990. Just a few years after the birth of their children, Conrad and Hefner divorced in 1998. Eventually, in 2009 Conrad filed for divorce, which was finalized in 2010.

Hugh married for the third time to model and TV personality Crystal Harris. They walked down the aisle in 2012 and remained married until he died in 2017.

Death of Millie Williams’ Ex-Hubby Hugh Hefner

The successful American publisher Hugh died on 27 September 2017 at his home in Holmby Hills, Los Angeles, California, aged 91. He reportedly died of sepsis caused by an E. coli infection.

His body was buried next to Marilyn Monroe in Westwood Memorial Park in Los Angeles. In 1992, he had reportedly paid $75,000 to reserve a place next to the deceased Monroe. He told the Los Angeles Times in 2009.

Spending eternity next to Marilyn is an opportunity too sweet to pass up

Is Hugh Hefner’s First Wife Still Alive?

Well, Millie is still alive. She will be 95 in 2021 and is reported to be active. However, she has completely avoided public attention. She does not even participate in social media platforms, so it is difficult to keep track of her life at the moment.

However, we hope to see her in the coming days.

Millie Hefner’s Net Worth

As Hugh’s ex-wife, Millie may have inherited a good fortune as part of the divorce settlement. But unfortunately, she has never disclosed the exact amount of her net worth to the media. However, as far as Hugh is concerned, his fortune at the time of his death in 2017 was approximately $45 million.
