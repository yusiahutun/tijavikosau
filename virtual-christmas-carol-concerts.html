6 of The Best Virtual Christmas Carol Concerts

July 2024 · 7 minute read

Christmas carol concerts are among the many highlights of the festive season – and this year, lots are virtual. While many are hoping to meet in person, lots of churches, charities and organisations are still streaming their much-loved carol services to offer something for everyone.

Whether you prefer traditional or contemporary, there is plenty to get you into the festive spirit. Grab a mince pie and take look at the best uplifting virtual carol concerts to tune into...

1. Salisbury Cathedral

Looking for a Christmas Day service you can enjoy at home? Well, Salisbury Cathedral will be airing its Carols at the Cathedral on BBC Radio Wiltshire on Christmas Eve and Christmas morning. If you're unable to attend in person (the service will be recorded on Sunday 12th December 2021), then make sure to tune in online.

Tickets: Free
Date: 24th and 25th December 2021
Time: TBC

MORE INFO

2. Carol concert by Marie Curie

Charity Marie Curie is kicking off the festive season with an online carol concert for all to enjoy at home. The online service, Carols in the City at Home, will be streamed online on Wednesday 8th December following on from the success of last year.

There's lots to look forward to including performances by the chamber choir Canticum and seasonal readings by celebrity guests, so you really don't want to miss this.

Tickets: Free
Date: Wednesday 8th December 2021
Time: 19:00pm

MORE INFO

Alicia Llop//Getty Images

3. Virtual carols by the Alzheimer's Society

To help boost the spirits of the nation, the Alzheimer's Society is hosting a wonderful evening of virtual festivities on Wednesday 15th December. Expect Christmas songs the whole family will love, plus a Grace Dent as the host and Britain's Got Talent Semi-finalists, Shalom Chorale, as the choir.

Tickets: £10 online, or £45 to attend in person
Date: Wednesday 15th December 2021
Time: 19:45pm

MORE INFO

4. Supporting Wounded Veterans (SWV) carol concert

Streamed straight to your home live from St Columba's in London, expect an uplifting and beautiful service filled with singers, musicians, readers and SWV Veterans. While tickets are not yet on sale, make sure to pencil this one in your diary.

Speaking about last year's concert, SWV say: "We are SO delighted with how many of you joined us for our virtual carol concert. At last count, over 750 families and friends have enjoyed our wonderful cast of singers, readers, veterans and soloists. We have received so many lovely comments matched by equally lovely (and generous) donations and we cannot thank you enough!"

Tickets: TBC
Date: Tuesday 7th December 2021
Time: TBC

MORE INFO

NurPhoto//Getty Images

5. Childline's Merry Little Christmas concert

The second virtual edition of Childline's annual Merry Little Christmas concert, this beautiful livestream will be filled with celebrity readings, spectacular musical performances, and magical entertainment for everyone to enjoy at home.

Sponsored by Good Housekeeping, there's also a silent auction to help you get a head start on your Christmas shopping, while also raising vital funds for Childline.

Tickets: £20 per household (virtual) or £40 per person for in-person tickets
Date: Thursday 9th December 2021
Time: 19:00pm

MORE INFO

6. Carers Trust online Carol Concert

After a successful concert last year, this year will feature beautiful seasonal music from the acclaimed City of London Choir and celebrity readings. There's also the option to attend in person at St George's Hanover Square, London.

For more information about this year's event, please contact the charity.

Tickets: TBC
Date: Wednesday 1st December 2021
Time: TBC
