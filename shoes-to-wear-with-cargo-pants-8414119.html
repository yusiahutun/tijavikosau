11 Shoes to Wear With Cargo Pants

August 2024 · 4 minute read

If you’ve been keeping your eye on the latest trends, then it will come as no surprise that cargo pants are everywhere right now. A stylish alternative to jeans and uncomfortable slacks, cargos are the perfect staple for your winter wardrobe. Also, did we mention the pockets?

Celebs have been embracing these stylish pants for months now—just take a look at Kim Kardashian and Hailey Bieber—likely due to the resurgence of Y2K styles. With a looser fit and utilitarian style, cargos provide a fresh and modern take on the ever-popular classic.

With endless options to choose from, finding the perfect shoe pairing can often be difficult. Whether you’re into chunky boots or cozy slippers, we’ve got ideas for every style. 

Simple Sneakers

Looking for something comfy that never goes out of style? For the ultimate street-style look, try a casual sneaker like Nike Dunks. Though more masculine than other styles, it will transform your ensemble from casual to cool-girl in an instant.

Pointed Pumps

With oversized pants comes extra length, which can be a bit of a hassle when trudging through winter weather, or if you’re simply on the shorter side. If you’re in need of a bit of a lift, pairing your cargos with a pointed pump is a must.

Square-Toe Boots

Bella Hadid styled her denim cargo pants with a square-toe boot and we are obsessed. Proving that black boots reign supreme for winter wardrobe stylings, these chunky shoes give you enough height to keep your pants from dragging while simultaneously providing a chic alternative to the traditional pointed heel.

Classic Combat Boots

For an option you likely already have in your closet and are dying to break out again, combat boots make for a flawlessly edgy style. Try adding a plain black top and leather jacket to go all-in on the edge, or simply style with a graphic tee for something more laid back.

Block Heels

Block heels come in a variety of styles, and they are the perfect match to give you some extra height when dealing with an oversized cargo. Best yet? The style pairings are endless—you can choose something more comfortable by throwing on a chunky boot or go all-in with a stunning platform pump.

Cowboy Boots

Hear us out on this one. Cowboy boots may not be for everyone, but you can’t deny they make for a killer statement piece. For the girl who won’t shy away from the unconventional, try pairing with a straight-leg cargo to tuck easily into the boot for a runway-inspired look.

Flatform Sandals

Embrace the Y2K trend by pairing your cargo pants by pairing with flatform sandals. Our tip: Make sure you pick a flatform with a thick enough sole to make a statement and peek out below your pants, since cargos can be notoriously baggy.

Ugg Mules

There is nothing comfier than a fluffy slipper on a cold winter day. Great news: you don’t have to sacrifice that for a chic outfit anymore. When paired with your cargo pants, Ugg mules are the perfect laid-back accessory for the ultimate model-off-duty ensemble. Try layering a longline jacket over a comfy statement sweater to really level up your fashion game.

Trendy Slides

For quickly stepping out of your apartment, slides paired with a slim-fit cargo gives the ultimate it-girl aesthetic. We know, wearing slides during winter can get a bit cold, but pair it with your comfiest oversized sweater and you’re ready to hit the town.

Knee-High Rain Boots

Equestrian style will never go out of fashion. When looking for a more sophisticated ensemble, balance out your cargos with a pair of knee-high chunky rain boots to emulate this style.

Ankle Boots

Don’t underestimate the ankle boot. Pairing your cargo pants with a suede or leather ankle boot is always a good idea, lending a clean, put-together style. Ideal for office days, you can pair with a statement jacket so everyone knows exactly who is in charge.
