Influencer gets cast of boobs to 'celebrate stretch marks, scars and the girls'

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Influencer Nelly London has showed off her 'amazing' boob cast to fans.

The body positive influencer took to her Instagram page to share the metallic cast of her chest with her 466,000 followers.

And she couldn't help but rave about how great it was.

Nelly, who is still recovering from boob reduction surgery she had done eight years ago, was seen posing happily with the cast on her Instagram grid as she held it up to her chest.

She told fans: "This has been the best experience and I am so glad I decided to get my boobs cast after years of thinking about it.

"It’s the nicest way to celebrate the girls as they are right now, especially as my next surgery is only a couple of days away."

The social media star also took to her Instagram story to give her followers more details on the cast.

Nelly wrote: "I can't believe that's me. It's so beautiful.

"Look at the detail, the stretch marks, the tiny mole, the scars, I'm speechless."

She then added: "I am so incredibly happy I did this after years of putting it off because of scarring.

"The scars are the bits that look the most beautiful! We made art!"

Nelly is still undergoing surgery to correct the boob reduction she had eight years ago.

She decided to undergo the surgery at the time as she felt very self conscious about her HH boobs that made her life difficult.

However, she was horrified with the aftermath.

Nelly previously told BBC Three : “When I found out what breast reduction surgery was, I booked to see a surgeon that week and had the surgery the week after.

“So I really went in hard and fast...

“About four or five days after the surgery, I felt a ping under my left boob."

Nelly added: "I wasn’t supposed to but I took off the dressings to have a look at what had happened.

"It looked like a stitch had popped and my breast was bulging through a hole in the bottom of my boob.”

The Instagram star said she was in a lot of pain but the surgeon reassured her.

However, sadly her problem went from bad to worse as more openings popped across her skin.

Nelly was told her body was rejecting the internal stitches, so she had to have them removed.

The surgeon didn’t want to operate on her again, so she had to let them heal.

Nelly spent eight months in recovery and had to be hospitalised a couple of times a week to get the wound re-dressed.

She went on to have corrective surgery to help reduce the scarring on her body – but even now, she’s still healing from the operation.

However, she has learned to embrace her surgery scars, sayingL “I now take really great pride in showing off my scars. I take a lot of pictures of them and put them on Instagram.

“I am openly proud of them because they’ve been such a journey for me.”
