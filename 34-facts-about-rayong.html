Beautiful Beaches

Rayong is home to some of the most stunning beaches in Thailand, including Mae Ramphueng Beach, Suan Son Beach, and Laem Mae Phim Beach.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Rayong is steeped in history and has a rich cultural heritage. The city has numerous temples and historical sites that offer a glimpse into its past.

Delicious Seafood

Rayong is famous for its fresh and delicious seafood. From grilled prawns to steamed fish, visitors can indulge in a wide variety of mouthwatering dishes.

Fruit Paradise

Rayong is known as the fruit paradise of Thailand. The city is surrounded by fruit orchards, where visitors can enjoy picking and tasting a wide range of tropical fruits.

Charming Fishing Villages

Rayong is dotted with picturesque fishing villages, where visitors can immerse themselves in the local way of life and witness traditional fishing practices.

Vibrant Nightlife

Rayong offers a vibrant nightlife scene with a range of bars, clubs, and night markets. Visitors can enjoy live music, delicious street food, and a lively atmosphere.

Home to National Parks

Rayong is home to several national parks, including Khao Laem Ya – Mu Ko Samet National Park and Khao Chamao – Khao Wong National Park, where visitors can explore and experience the beauty of nature.

Ceramic Industry

Rayong is known for its thriving ceramic industry. The city produces a wide range of beautiful ceramics, including pottery, sculptures, and decorative items.

Eco-Friendly Destination

Rayong is committed to sustainable tourism practices and is known for its eco-friendly initiatives, including conservation projects and eco-tours.

Rayong Marathon

Rayong hosts an annual marathon that attracts runners from around the world. The event takes place along the scenic coastal roads, offering participants breathtaking views.

Water Activities

With its crystal-clear waters and abundant marine life, Rayong is a paradise for water sports enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy snorkeling, diving, kayaking, and fishing.

Famous Fireworks Festival

Rayong hosts a spectacular fireworks festival known as the Rayong International Fireworks Festival. The event showcases dazzling displays that light up the sky.

Traditional Thai Massage

Rayong is the perfect place to indulge in traditional Thai massage and spa treatments. Visitors can relax and rejuvenate in the hands of skilled therapists.

Tropical Fruit Orchards

Rayong’s tropical climate is ideal for fruit cultivation, and the region is known for its abundant fruit orchards. Visitors can taste delicious mangoes, durians, and rambutans.

Affordable Accommodation

Rayong offers a range of accommodation options to suit every budget, from luxury resorts to budget-friendly guesthouses and homestays.

Chemical Industry Hub

Rayong is a major hub for the chemical and petrochemical industry in Thailand. The city is home to numerous industrial estates and factories.

Natural Hot Springs

Rayong is blessed with natural hot springs, where visitors can soak in mineral-rich waters and enjoy the therapeutic benefits.

Traditional Floating Market

Rayong’s floating market offers a unique shopping experience, with vendors selling fresh produce, handicrafts, and local delicacies from their boats.

Stunning Sunsets

Rayong boasts breathtaking sunsets, painting the sky in vibrant hues of orange and pink. The beaches provide the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable sunset view.

Scenic Golf Courses

Rayong is a haven for golf enthusiasts, with several world-class golf courses offering stunning views and challenging fairways.

Silk Weaving Tradition

FAQs

1. What is the best time to visit Rayong?

The best time to visit Rayong is during the dry season, which lasts from November to February. The weather is pleasant, and the skies are clear, making it ideal for outdoor activities.

2. How do I get to Rayong?

You can reach Rayong by flying into Suvarnabhumi Airport in Bangkok and then taking a taxi or bus to Rayong, which is approximately a two-hour drive away.

3. What are some popular attractions in Rayong?

Some popular attractions in Rayong include Hat Mae Ramphueng (Mae Ramphueng Beach), Rayong Aquarium, Suan Son (Pine Park), and Wat Pa Pradu.

4. Are there any outdoor activities in Rayong?

Absolutely! Rayong offers a wide range of outdoor activities such as snorkeling, diving, kayaking, hiking, and visiting national parks like Khao Laem Ya – Mu Ko Samet National Park.

5. Can I try local cuisine in Rayong?

Yes, Rayong is known for its delicious seafood. You can indulge in fresh and mouthwatering seafood dishes at the local restaurants and night markets.

6. Is Rayong a family-friendly destination?

Yes, Rayong is a family-friendly destination with plenty of options for kids. You can visit Samet Island, enjoy water sports, or have a picnic at one of the many parks in the city.

7. Are there any accommodations options available in Rayong?

Yes, Rayong offers a range of accommodation options to suit every budget, from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly hotels and guesthouses.

8. What are some nearby attractions that can be visited from Rayong?

From Rayong, you can explore nearby attractions such as Pattaya, Koh Samet, Koh Chang, and Chanthaburi, each offering its own unique attractions and experiences.
