A 60-Year-Old Shopper Said Their Skin Look Youthful Thanks to This Serum

July 2024 · 2 minute read

One thing I’ve learned from speaking with my elders and reading hundreds of product reviews every week is that the sooner you extend your skincare routine past the end of the chin, the better. It’s (seemingly) easier to plump the skin on your face than it is to counteract the effects of gravity on your jowls and neck. You have your work cut out for you when it comes to picking through options but a product that stays top of mind is SBLA’s Neck, Chin, and Jawline Sculpting Wand. 

If you follow 70-year-old supermodel Christie Brinkley on Instagram, you’ve probably come across SBLA before (she was a brand ambassador for a few years). It started when, according to SBLA’s website, she was so impressed with the products that she reached out to the brand herself. The brand makes large anti-aging serum dispensing wands for different parts of the face. Its hero product is the Firm, Lift, Sculpt, and Define Neck, Chin, and Jawline Sculpting Wand which has 900-plus five-star ratings across Amazon and its website (where it’s currently on sale). 

There is no guesswork here; a single push of the button on the wand dispenses the precise amount of serum you need. As you apply it over the relevant areas, you’ll get both instant and long-term results. The former is mostly a result of the metal ball applicator which is cooling, depuffing, and circulation-boosting. SBLA’s Neck, Chin, and Jawline Sculpting Wand purportedly firms, lifts, sculpts, and defines. It’s an almost unreasonably tall order that is actually plausible once you take a look at both the comprehensive formula and Christie Brinkley’s ageless face. 

The key players are a hyaluronic acid complex, peptide blend, and acetyl glucosamine (amino acid). They pack a punch: gentle exfoliation, tighter, plumper, firmer, more moisturized, less wrinkled, de-puffed, and more even-toned skin. 

A 60-year-old shopper said SBLA’s Sculpting Wand has taken “a decade or more off [their] neck.” They said that it “firmed” and “tightened” their skin in addition to improving their “sagging jowls.” Another reviewer was “shocked by the improvement” they saw in their hereditary “jowls and neck sagging.” 

Shop the Neck, Chin, and Jawline Sculpting Wand on sale at SBLA, or at full price on Amazon.
