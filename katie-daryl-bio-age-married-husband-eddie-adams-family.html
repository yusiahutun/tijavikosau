Katie Daryl Bio, Age, Married, Husband Eddie Adams, Family

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Katie Daryl is a fast-growing television presenter and producer who is constantly on her way to the top of the American media industry. As a teenager, she worked as a radio DJ and worked her way through the ranks to become a radio producer. She continued on this path during her studies and switched to television after graduation.

Daryl gained some recognition through her work on the Hollywood gossip television show TMZ on television, the television version of the popular celebrity news website TMZ. After her brilliant work on TMZ, she landed a much bigger gig on AXS TV as host of the music show “The World’s Greatest Tribute Bands”. The multi-talented TV star has recently become known for her work on the popular music countdown series The Top Ten Revealed, which is also broadcast on the AXS TV network.

Katie Daryl Biography and Age

Katie Daryl was born on June 7, 1984. Information about her early life and education is scarce, but she has revealed that her first experience in the media was working as a part-time radio DJ when she was a teenager.

After graduating from high school, she attended Arizona State University for her college education. During her studies, she continued to gain experience in the media industry, especially radio. She worked as a morning show producer for KISS-FM in Phoenix, Arizona. In 1998, she completed an internship with the Sports Fan Radio Network under the auspices of the Association for Women in Sports Media.

Due to the high demands of working on the morning radio shows, Katie Daryl decided to switch to television after graduating from college. She also moved to New York City, where she got a job as a correspondent on the political show Choose or Lose on MTV. She then moved to Dallas, Texas, where she briefly worked at FM radio.

Daryl’s first major television job was co-hosting the Mark Cuban Show on UPN and CBS. She hosted the show for two seasons alongside Mark Cuban. In the early 2000s, she joined HDNet, a cable television station founded by Mark Cuban. Her first role was that of host and producer of the channel’s music show True Music. She also hosted another show Zippo Hot Tour in 2005, and the following year she began hosting Deadline! Her role as host of Deadline! lasted 6 seasons between 2006 and 2012 and on July 2, 2012, HDNet was renamed AXS TV.

In early 2013, Katie Daryl received her biggest role to date in the music show The World’s Greatest Tribute Bands. She had a highly successful 8 seasons as host and executive producer of the show between 2013 and 2017 and her brilliant work on the show earned her the CableFAX Program Award for “Best Host – Music” in 2017. In addition to her duties with The World’s Greatest Tribute Bands, she also hosted several other shows on AXS TV.

Also in 2017, the multi-talented TV star began creating an original music countdown series. The series, entitled The Top Ten Revealed, premiered in February 2018 and features a panel of music experts and rock stars discussing a wide range of music topics. After an extremely successful debut season, the show was extended for a second season in November 2018.
