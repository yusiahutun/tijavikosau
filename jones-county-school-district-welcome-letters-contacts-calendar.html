East Jones Elementary

Sylvia Busby

East Jones Elementary is ready to welcome all Tiger Cubs “Back to the Jungle” on Monday, August 4, 2014, for our annual Open House. The school will be open from 5:00 P.M. to 7:00 P.M. to allow parents and students the opportunity to visit classrooms and bring supplies. The first day of school will be Wednesday, August 6.

Both of the East Jones Elementary Administrators received promotions with Mrs. Sylvia Busby being named Supervising Principal and Mr. Mike Lightsey as Assistant Principal. Both bring numerous years of supervisory experience with the Jones County School District.

Administrators, faculty, and staff have been diligently preparing to welcome students to the 2014 – 2015 school year. The diligent work that has been taking place is very evident when walking down any hallway or entering any classroom. One would have no doubt that our theme for the school year is “Readin’, Writin’,’ Rithmetic, Round Up.” Yes, our students will be honoring the basic skills and applying them to Common Core Standards.

Our school will be open each day from 8:00 A. M. – 3:00 P. M. for parents to come by and pick up the new registration packets for this year. We are requesting two current proofs of residency. If you have any questions, please call our office at 601-425-9799. We look forward to a GREAT 2014 – 2015 school year as we begin classes in the Jungle.

Sylvia Busby East Jones Elementary, Supervising Principal 

South Jones Elementary

South Jones welcomes our students back to school. We hope it has been an fun and eventful summer for our students.

We are proud of our students and the efforts they gave last year in their studies. We wish to continue that positive trend for this new school year.

We wish to encourage our parents to be involved with their children’s education this new school year. Our Parent Teacher Organization always sets a great pace in helping our school make meaningful improvements.

Please be aware our open house will be on Aug. 4. Jones County Schools begin on Aug. 6. Again, welcome to our new school year.

Steve Musgrove,

South Jones Elementary, Principal 

Glade Elementary

Lisa Ishee

It is with great pleasure and honor that I welcome our students, parents, faculty and staff back to Glade Elementary for another exciting and successful school year. The teachers and I are eagerly awaiting the arrival of all our amazing students on Aug. 6. In preparation for the new year, teachers have begun preparing for the New Common Core State Standards by attending professional development trainings as well as creatively decorating and organizing their classrooms.

Whether you are joining us for the first time this year or you have been with us before, we encourage all of our families to be active members in supporting education. In addition to our excellent academic program, we offer many activities for our students and families to participate in throughout the year.

A “Commitment to Excellence” is our promise to you and my expectations of every student. We look forward to this school year with great excitement and know all of our students will have a rewarding and successful school year. Please join us for Open House on Monday, Aug. 4 from 5-7: p.m.

We’re “Being Unusually Good – BUG” as we wait for your arrival and are looking forward to seeing you all really soon!

Mrs. Lisa Ishee Glade Elementary, Principal 

West Jones Elementary

I would like to take a moment to welcome everyone to a new school year! This year will be filled with opportunities for everyone. There will be new things to learn, friends to make and activities to be involved in for all students. From kindergarten to seniors, classes will be buzzing with energy!

Here at WJE, teachers, principals and all the staff have been working hard to have everything in place to make the best possible school year. We look forward to renewing old friendships and making new ones. As you return to school this year, do so with great expectations! We will do our best to help you achieve them.

Mark Reddoch

West Jones Elementary, Principal

Moselle Elementary

Edward Crosby

On behalf of the faculty and staff of Moselle Elementary, we would like to take this opportunity to welcome all of our students back to school. We are proud of our past accomplishments and looking forward to another successful year.

Our students at Moselle Elementary are our first priority. We believe that our students, with the guidance from parents and teachers, can accomplish anything.

We continue to ask for prayers for the Moselle students, faculty and staff. With God’s help, we will strive to inspire our students to be the best they can be.

West Jones High 

Lynn Lyon

West Jones High School is pleased to welcome returning and new students for the 2014-2015 school year. Students in grades 7-12 will encounter several new faces on our faculty and staff and many changes in the subjects being offered as we implement Common Core State Standards as the basis of our curriculum. Registration will take place on July 24, 25 and 28. We will have school-wide open house on Aug. 5 from 5:30- 7:30 p.m., and school beings Aug. 6.

We look forward to a great year and “Go Mustangs.”

Lynn Lyon

West Jones High, Supervising Principal

Jones County Career and Technical Center

Patsy Reon

The 2014-15 school year is gearing up to be a great year for Career & Technical students. The following classes are offered to students in grades 10-12 from Northeast Jones High School, South Jones High School and West Jones High School: Architecture and Drafting, Culinary Arts, Health Science, Teacher Academy, Welding, Automotive Repair, Collision Repair

Other Career & Technical courses offered at each school campus are: Agriculture and Family and Consumer a Science.

Career Pathway Experience (students are employed) STEM Technology Foundations

Patsy Reon,

Jones County Career and Technical Center, Director
