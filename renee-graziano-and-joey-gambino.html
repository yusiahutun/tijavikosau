About

Renee Graziano is a 55 year old American Reality TV Contestant. Born on 12th July, 1968 in Staten Island, New York City, New York, USA, she is famous for being the daughter of Anthony Graziano, a former consigliere of the Bonanno crime family and her roles in two reality television shows, Mob Wives and Celebrity Big Brother in a career that spans 2010–present. Her zodiac sign is Cancer.
