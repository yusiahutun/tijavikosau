Most shocking celeb weight loss transformations of 2023 revealed including Kelly Clarkson and Tammy

September 2024 · 12 minute read

SEVERAL celebrities have undergone weight loss transformations throughout the past year.

Fans watched as Kelly Clarkson's figure shrunk a bit more each day on her talk show and witnessed the results of TLC star Tammy Slaton's life-changing bariatric surgery among others.

Here are the most shocking celebrity weight loss transformations fans witnessed in 2023.

TAMMY SLATON

When 1000-Lb. Sisters debuted in 2020, Tammy, 37, weighed 605 pounds and after finding out how difficult weight loss can be, struggled to shed the extra pounds to reach her goal weight.

In Season 2, fans learned that the reality TV star was hospitalized after contracting Covid-19.

While she had lost about 55 pounds before the pandemic started, she gained almost all of it back when she got sick.

Post-recovery, Tammy continued to gain weight until she weighed a whopping 717 pounds.

Last summer, however, Tammy managed to reach her goal weight which enabled her to get bariatric surgery, and was shown standing and walking around — tasks she previously had trouble with.

Tammy's current weight has been revealed in court papers after her drastic body transformation.

The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed that she weighed 448 pounds as of August 4, 2023.

RIDING SHOTGUN

Tammy was shown being able to sit in the front seat of a car for the first time in over two decades during the most recent episode of 1000-Lb. Sisters.

The 37-year-old was about to travel with her brother Chris Combs and before they got to the car, he announced that he was going to try to put Tammy in the front seat.

In response, Tammy was concerned that she wouldn't be able to fit in between the door and the console in the car.

To which Chris said: "We can squish. As long as the door shuts, we're good," with a laugh.

In a confessional, she admitted that she hadn't sat in the front seat of a car since she was 15 or 16 years old.

"It's been 20 years," Tammy revealed but still, the TV star was willing to give it a shot.

With little effort, she used her walker to get to the car and then gently positioned herself in the front seat.

When she realized she would fit, Tammy had a huge smile plastered across her face.

In confessional, Chris said: "When Tammy realizes that she sat down in the chair and she's good to go and the door can close, you can tell the little battle that she just beat made her feel wonderful."

KELLY CLARKSON

Earlier this week, Kelly, 41, showed off her impressive figure on her talk show once more after losing 50 pounds.

The American Idol winner was featured on the Instagram account for The Kelly Clarkson Show as she showed off her dramatic weight loss.

The Grammy-winning artist has been flaunting her slimmer frame in a variety of red and green outfits on her show in recent weeks.

For Tuesday's show, Kelly was shown announcing show guests Sydney Sweeney, 26, and Da'Vine Joy Randolph, 37.

The three women stood next to each other in holiday attire, with the Stronger singer declaring: "Everyone got the memo today on Kelly!"

In the middle, Kelly rocked a green velvet pantsuit that featured black tiger stripes throughout.

The outfit featured a plunging V-neckline and a tie at the waist, cinching in Kelly's tiny midsection.

The singer had her hair tied half up and opted for a red lip color and platform heels to finish her Christmas look.

Fans in the comments section hyped up Kelly's new figure, with one writing: "These looks for Kelly have been [fire]."

"Kelly has ALWAYS been beautiful, she looks amazing and most importantly, happy," a second declared.

A third similarly wrote: "Kelly looks amazing."

In a recent interview with a physician who specializes in nutritional medicine, The U.S. Sun learned how much weight Kelly has lost this year.

Comparing photos from March 2023 of Kelly at the iHeartRadio Music Awards and photos from November 2023 of the star at a SiriusXM event, Dr. Fred Pescatore believes she lost 50 pounds.

Based on the photos alone, he noticed a significant transformation in "her hands, face, and her breast size."

While the talk show host has not admitted to using Ozempic or any similar drugs, Dr. Pescatore, who has not treated nor has any ties to Kelly, suggested the use of it.

"It is possible she is on a diet, but all things point to Ozempic or Mounjaro," he argued, referencing the before and after photos.

The U.S. Sun has reached out to Kelly's rep for comment.

When asked if it could be something she's ashamed of admitting publicly, Dr. Pescatore said that sometimes celebrities "do extreme things and don't want the world to know."

He added: "In her case, she is a talk show host and has to be relatable.

"Taking a drug that is $1,000 per month isn't very relatable to the women who watch her show."

While it is unclear whether or not Kelly has been using the drug, after shedding serious weight over the past few months, she revealed that she has loved the process of slimming down.

As the Since U Been Gone singer spoke with sisters Barbara, 41, and Jenna Bush Hager, 41, on her show, the trio delved into the topic of not fitting into their clothes anymore.

Kelly stated: "Ugh, I don't fit into mine. I love losing weight but here's the thing. Jeans are so hard, I feel like when you have a butt and a smaller waist."

She also recently bragged about no longer having to wear Spanx.

On an episode of The Kelly Clarkson Show, she revealed: "I don't even need to wear Spanx anymore, I just wear them for warmth like thermals."

Kelly then added, "Life is good" after once more confirming that she no longer needed to wear the garment.

'UNBOTHERED'

Over the summer, Emily sparked concern after she posted a video from her appearance on Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen.

The star posed on the red carpet in a skintight jumpsuit by Nadine Merabi, which emphasized her much slimmer frame as she showed her body from every angle.

She captioned the Instagram post: "Unbothered Barbie."

In the comment section, fans were quick to show concern over Emily's drastic - and quick - weight loss.

"All of them have the same Ozempic body..." one follower wrote.

"She had a body transplant," a second jokingly claimed.

A third said: "Umm that’s an unhealthy weight loss really fast?" to which Emily quickly snapped back: "Ummm it wasn’t fast. We filmed this season a year ago."

While many followers noted the fact that Emily was rocking an "Ozempic body," the TV star has nothing to hide when it comes to the weight management medicine that has taken Hollywood by storm.

She stated that after filming wrapped, she had a doctor's appointment, where the idea of taking Ozempic was put on the table.

“At that time I didn’t even know what Ozempic was honestly,” she told Us Weekly.

“The doctor was just like, ‘Oh, there’s this new weight loss drug and it’s just been FDA approved and it’s great.

"I think it would really help you lose some weight and you’ll feel better and you’ll be able to get working out again and feeling more like yourself.’ And I was like, ‘OK, sounds great.’

"Anyway, I did that in December. It was great for my diet. It really just kickstarted me into eating better and eating healthier ’cause it makes you feel like crap ’cause nothing tastes good.”

Emily also received liposuction and said she changed up her diet and exercise routine to further lose weight.

“I cut out a lot of things and I go to the gym seven days a week and work out. I work out like I’m training for a fitness competition. … That’s kind of the whole story,” she said.

“The thing that bothers me the most is that when people comment on the way that I look, they constantly say, ‘Ozempic, Ozempic.’
