Josh Popper is an American boxer instructor, footballer, entrepreneur, and social media personality. Josh Popper is the founder of the Bredwinners boxing gym in New York City. He came to the news after being rumoured to be the boyfriend of singer and songwriter Madonna.

Early Life

Josh Popper was born on September 24, 1993, in Egg Harbor Township, New Jersey, United States. Currently, he is 31 years old as of 2024, and he has a birth sign of Libra.

He is an American by nationality and belongs to the African-American ethnicity. Additionally, he practices Christianity religion. Currently, he resides in Manhattan,  New York City, New York.

The names of his parents are Michael Popper Sr. (father) and Vanita Allen (mother). Meanwhile, his mother is an entrepreneur and shares a close bond with his mother.

He was raised along with his siblings; Michael, Popper, Chris J Popper, Michael J. Popper Jr. Michael J. Popper Jr, and Justina Popper. His mother raised her children single-handedly.

Josh Popper – Education

In terms of his academics, he attended Holy Spirit High School. After completing his high school education, he enrolled at Rowan University, Glassboro, New Jersey studied there from 2011 to 2015.

Professional Career

Josh Popper began his career in football before moving on to boxing. During his sophomore year of college in 2011, he recorded 22 tackles, three for loss, two sacks, and even an interception. In 2013, he was chosen to the NJAC All-Conference first team and started all 12 games.

Furthermore, he recorded 65 tackles, 11.5 for loss, 2.5 sacks, two interceptions, one of which he recovered for a score, and two defensive passes. He attempted to join the National Football League (NFL). However, he was never drafted by any NFL team.
