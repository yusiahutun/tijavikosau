Diane Kathleen Murphy (born June 17, 1964) is a former child actress, best known for sharing the role of "Tabitha" with her fraternal twin sister Erin in the 1960s television series Bewitched for one season. During the first year or two, Diane shared the role with Erin, until their looks started to differ. After the first year or two, Diane was eased out and Erin took over the part for the remaining six seasons of Bewitched.
