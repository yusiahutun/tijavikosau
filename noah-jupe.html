Noah Jupe Bio, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth, Wiki

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Noah Jupe is a popular and hardworking English actor who is known for his role in “A Quiet Place”, “Honey Boy” and “Wonder”. He has also appeared in a music video named “From Chaos to Harmony” by artist Ian Brown. His parents have a background working in the film industry and from a very young age, he wanted to become an actor. He works really hard to give his best performance in every possible scene.

Early Life

Noah Jupe was born on 25th February 2005 in Islington, London, United Kingdom. His mother’s name is Katy Cavanagh who is an actress known for her role in “Coronation Street” a British TV Show. Chris Jupe is his father who is a filmmaker, and owner of Drive Productions and has also partnered with Funky Films which is also a video production company. He has a sister named Jemma Jupe and a brother named Jacobi Jupe. He is currently attending Cheadle Hulme School at Cheadle Hulme, Stockport, England.

Noah Jupe Career

His career was started in 2015 and it was easy for him to work in the film industry because of his family’s history. But he did not take the advantage of the same instead he gave his performance in films or TV shows. In 2015 he worked on three TV shows named Downtown Abbey, Penny Dreadful, and A Song for Jenny. In 2016 he again worked on three TV shows named Houdini &Doyle, The Last Dragonslayer, and The Night Manger. After that in 2017, he worked in a film named “Wonder” where his work was highly appreciated by people all over the world. He also worked in other films named The man with the Iron Heart, That Good Night, and Suburbicon.

In 2018 he further made an appearance in a thriller film called “The Quiet Place” which received a lot of positive reviews. He appeared in the film with John Krasinski and Emily Blunt. The other films where he worked are Holmes & Watson and The Titan. In 2019 he made an appearance in a music video named “From Chaos to Harmony” by artist Ian Brown. Apart from a music video he also worked in a film called Ford V Ferrari and Honey Boy. On 25th October 2020, he was seen in a TV series named “The Undoing” where his role was of Henry Fraser. In 2021 “A Quiet Place Part II” will be released and currently he is filming for another film called “No Sudden Move”.

Awards and Nominations

In 2018 he has been nominated for London Film Critics Circle for Young British Performer of the Year, Los Angeles Online Film Critics Society for Best Performer by an Actor 23 and Under, and Young Artist Awards for Best Performer in a Feature Film. In 2019 he won Hollywood Critics Association Awards for Honey Boy under the category of Best Performer by an Actor 23 and under.

Further, he was nominated for the Choice Movie Award for Best Young Actor, Gotham Independent Film Awards for Breakthrough Actor, and London Film Critics Circle for the category Young British Performer of the Year. In 2020 he was nominated for London Film Critics Circle for Young British Performer of the Year and Independent Spirit Award for Best Supporting Male.
