15 best Korean games app for Android and iOS devices in 2024

April 2024 · 7 minute read

South Korea is one of the leading markets in mobile games worldwide. At least 70% of South Koreans play mobile games on Android and iOS devices. But have you ever wondered what types of games users play in South Korea? Generally, many South Koreans prefer MMORPG (massively multiplayer online role-playing games). However, there have also been a considerable number of players who play simulation and sports games. But which is the best Korean games app for Android and iOS in 2024?

RPGs (role-playing games) have dominated the South Korean gaming market for years. The best examples are Lineage M, Lineage 2M, and R2M. Initially, South Koreans played MMORPG games on their computers. However, most developers converted their games to accommodate mobile users.

While action games are a staple of most South Korean gamers, the racing game KartRider Rush+ has attracted over one million users. Other games that enjoy a large following include Genshin Impact, Cookie Run: OvernBreak, and Ninja Must Die.

15 best Korean games app for Android and iOS

You cannot fall short of a South Korean video game on your Android or iOS device. Here is a look at 15 of the best video games that Korean people play.

1. Blade & Soul: Revolution

Blade & Soul: Revolution is one of the best role-playing mobile games that Koreans play. Millions of Koreans love it because of its action-packed sequences. Also, players can decide to alter their appearances by choosing a unique character.

The game gets the props because of its unique storyline, artillery, and legendary rewards that make gameplay easier. The game is available for Android and iOS devices.

2. Cookie Run: OvenBreak

Cookie Run: OvenBreak is a free-to-play racing game. It allows you to play a cake-flavoured man who has to run away quickly from a witch’s oven. While running, you must also jump, slide, collect, and bake a cake to earn points.

The game is outstanding because of its challenging levels, fun gameplay, and huge rewards. Cookie Run: OvenBreak is available on Android and iOS.

3. Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is one of the best open-world action role-playing games. South Koreans love playing the game because it features an anime-style open-world environment. Furthermore, it has action-based battle systems that use character-switching and elemental magic.

Although Genshin Impact is free to play, it has an in-game currency that allows you to buy new weapons and characters. You can download the game for Android or iOS devices.

4. Lineage 2: Revolution

Lineage 2: Revolution is one of the best MMORPG online games popular in Korea and played on Android and iOS devices. The video game is part of the Lineage series, a medieval fantasy MMORPG.

In Lineage 2: Revolution, you choose a character and its race. Then, you progress by taking quests and killing mobs. You will encounter player versus player, clan wars, instanced dungeons, raids, and multiple quests.

5. Lineage W

Lineage W is also an MMORPG and part of the Lineage series. It is the fifth game in the Lineage series released on Android and iOS in 2021. The game takes its theme from Lineage II, whereby you create a character and begin to battle monsters.

Once you kill a monster, you get points that will help you level up and learn new skills. But if you die, you can choose where you want to resurrect.

6. Monster Super League

Monster Super League is a role-playing video game that transports you to a unique world called Leticia. However, the beautiful Leticia faces the challenge of chaos and imbalance.

Your task is to go to Leticia and restore balance. It may seem easy, but you will battle monsters that delight in disorder. The game is available on Android and iOS.

7. Mystic Messenger

Mystic Messenger is a story-based video game for Android and iOS targeted towards female gamers. First, a player chooses a female character and gives it a name.

The character then downloads an app that connects to a mystic group chat with attractive men. Thus, the story begins when the character joins the private party. Mystic Messenger gets high reviews because of its realism and simple plotline.

8. Ninja Must Die

Ninja Must Die is an ink-painting-style ninja real-time combat running game. It challenges the player to fight against bosses with exclusive skills and unravel the mysteries by solving puzzles to reveal the truth about the Ninja Realm.

The game gives you weapons and relics to strengthen your fights. You can download it for Android or iOS devices.

9. Odin: Valhalla Rising

Odin: Valhalla Raising is one of the best Korean game apps for Android. The MMORPG game introduces you to a mythical battlefield to win power and shining honour. However, you must be good at the game because you will face monsters and an overwhelming-scale boss raid as you try to grab power.

10. Rummikub

Rummikub follows the original version of the popular family game of the same name. In the gameplay, you only have to arrange the tiles to create unique colour and number combinations.

Rummikub is a game that requires tactical thinking and luck. But since you cannot play it alone, it makes the competition worthwhile. The game is available on Android and iOS.

11. Roblox

Roblox is an online game platform for Android and iOS. The unique thing about it is that it allows users to program or play games created by others.

The free-to-play gaming app enjoys a following of millions of gamers in South Korea and the rest of the world. Arsenal, Dead Ahead, and Zeppelin Wars are some of the most commonly played games in South Korea on the Roblox platform.

12. Summoners War: Sky Arena

Summoners War: Sky Arena is one of the most played games in Korea. The action-packed fantasy role-playing game enjoys a following of over 100 million players worldwide.

13. Survivor!.io

Survivor.io is a rogue-like adventure video game on Android and iOS played by millions of Koreans. Its gameplay involves a group of dangerous zombies invading a city and disrupting the peace.

Your task is to become a hero by saving the city. And although you have many weapons at your disposal, you are already outnumbered since you must battle over 1,000 zombies and exterminate them.

14. Phantomgate: The Last Valkyri

Phantomgate: The Last Valkyrie is an Android mystical adventure role-playing game. It introduces you to a fabled land called Midgard, where a mad god called Odin has held Astrid’s mother. Your work is to help Astrid, a daring young Valkyrie, save her mother and unravel the story of Astrid’s mysterious past.

KartRider Rush+ is one of the cute Korean game apps for Android and iOS devices. The free-to-play kart racing mobile game has attracted millions of players because of its high-octane racing action.

You can race with friends or play it alone through several gameplay modes while collecting and upgrading iconic karts and characters. The thrilling action makes the game worthwhile.
