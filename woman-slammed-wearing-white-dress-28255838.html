Woman slammed for wearing white dress to wedding fears she is 'terrible' person

July 2024 · 4 minute read

An influencer decided to wear a white dress to a wedding – and is now questioning whether she's a 'terrible' person.

Gabby Goessling wanted to match her outfit to her partner's so they'd look cute together on the big day.

The lad rocked a white shirt and a light green blazer, which meant the influencer hit colour coordination jackpot when she stumbled across the dress.

With the background of the garment primarily white, the strappy dress also had a beige and green flower design.

After she originally asked her 86,000 TikTok followers for their opinion, many urged her not to wear it in fears she would clash with the bride.

"Is this dress too white for a wedding?", she questioned. "I get so scared of being inappropriate and brought a spare just in case lol."

"Please tell me I'm not a terrible person", Gabby fretted in the caption of the follow up clip.

"I wore the dress to wedding and forgot about the TikTok completely.

The influencer gasped: "I woke up to so many comments that saying my dress was inappropriate and disrespectful."

"It's so bad, I feel so sh** about it because it apparently had too much white on it.

Another person slammed: "If you’re unsure if your dress is too white for a wedding, it usually is."

Meanwhile, a fifth user voiced: "Meh, depends on the overall vibe of the wedding. But I personally wouldn’t wear that to a wedding."
