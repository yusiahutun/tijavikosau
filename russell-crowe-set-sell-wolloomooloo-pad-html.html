Kiwi-Australian actor Russell Crowe is rumoured to be selling his lavish pad in Sydney's Woolloomooloo soon. 

According to The Sydney Morning Herald's Private Sydney, rumours are rife the 57-year-old will be putting the expansive waterfront property on the market and could very well fetch a whopping $40million for it. 

As the publication reports, Russell's pad features 11 bedrooms, having combined four properties in the complex, and even has its own 35-metre marina berth.

Russell first purchased the pad back in 2003 for $14.35million and is believed to have refused an offer of $25million for it back in 2017.
