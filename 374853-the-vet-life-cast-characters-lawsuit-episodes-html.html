Lawsuit

Despite successfully running an animal clinic, the three vets have not escaped lawsuits. In 2016, a family from Cypress sued the veterinarians who ran the Cy-Fair Animal Hospital for their dog's death.

Tony and Angela Grape claimed that hours after leaving their English bulldogs Zeus and Belvedere at the clinic, they got a call about Zeus having died from an anaesthesia administration.

The news ruined a trip they were to embark on. According to the family, their dogs had been dropped off at the facility for them to be neutered two days later. In their explanation, the dogs were not ready for surgery, considering that they had been fed and given water before being dropped off. Just like humans, they needed to have stayed a few hours without food before they could undergo any form of surgery.
