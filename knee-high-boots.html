How to style knee-high boots this season Best knee-high boots

Jimmy Choo

Jimmy Choo

Credit: Richard Valencia

Jimmy Choo's new statement 'drop heel' is their sexiest silhouette yet. Likened to a drop of liquid, its teardrop heel is a prime example of the fashion label's timeless design. It's perfect for party dressing this season.

Dear Frances

Dear Frances

A slouchy fit makes Dear Frances' knee-high boot the perfect candidate for evening footwear. Whether you're out to dinner with friends, drinking cocktails in your favourite bar or wearing with your go-to LBD at an elegant soirée, you can count on this style.

Khaite

When it comes to wardrobe staples, it's all in the details. The same rule applies when investing in a pair of knee-high boots – opt for a style that will go with everything but has subtle details to keep it interesting, like this angled heel and subtle cowboy boot
detailing.

Isabel Marant

Isabel Marant

If you're wanting to try out knee-high boots this winter, but don't feel brave enough to do so with a high heel, then opting for a flat is a great way to ease yourself in. These Isabel Marant boots offer a more casual but still luxurious approach.

Ganni

We've noticed more than a few fashionable women wearing this rubber style from Ganni, which is a stompier take on the knee-high boot. Prepare to add a little bit of edge to your feminine dresses or skirts and tights, thanks to contrast stitching and a chunky sole. Ideal for those rainy winter days, or even snow.

Paris Texas

Paris Texas

Different textures are a great way to invest in knee-high boots that feel unique but won't date easily. Crocodile leather (or crocodile-effect faux leather) is a timeless option, which can be worn with anything, from autumnal dresses and skirts to simple denim jeans.

Gucci

If you're more of a statement-making kind of person, then opting for a bold print may be more up your street. Something timeless like Gucci's classic GG motif truly never goes out of style and will add some oomph to any outfit.

Manu Atelier

Manu Atelier

High shine means high drama. As well as daring prints, there are other ways to be bold with your autumnal ensembles – turn to these statement-making boots by Manu Atelier, to put a sexy spin on a daytime look.

Arket

Investing in a lighter colour is a great way to elevate your winter wardrobe, which often tends to be made up of a darker colour palette. A nude or beige option can be softer, making it more casual and appropriate for the warmer season if you want to take your boots into the summer months.

Reformation

Reformation

These classic knee-high boots from Reformation feature a comfortable slim heel, so you can wear them all day and look stylish doing so. The pointed toes and wide silhouette make them the perfect option for wearing with tucked jeans or tights and short hemlines.
