Gucci Mane comes to agreement to pay $10K monthly in child support to son's mother Sheena Evans

August 2024 Â· 3 minute read

Gucci Mane has come to an accord with Sheena Evans, the mother of his son Keitheon, to pay her $10,000 monthly in child support.

Mane, whose real name is Radric Delantic Davis, and Evans told the presiding judge in their case in a Friday hearing that they agreed to the monthly figure, which the court put into record, The Blast reported.

The Birmingham, Alabama native, 39, also agreed to fund transportation expenses for Evans and their son, and pay her in excess of $100,000 for back child support he owed.

Evans has primary custody of the boy, and Mane will have scheduled visits, according to the website.

Evans, who welcomed the son with the rapper in 2007, took the rapper to court in 2018 to modify a 2011 custody agreement they hammered out in which she got primary custody of the boy, while Mane received visitation.

Under the terms of the 2011 pact, Mane was paying Evans a monthly support total of $2,026 based on an estimated income of $24,000 a month.

In returning to court last year, Evans sought to bump up Mane's monthly payments to $20,000, mentioning reports the rap artist plunked down more than $1 million for his October 2017 wedding to his spouse, Keyshia Ka'Oir, 34; Mane denied spending that much.

Evans also informed the court of 'significant change in circumstances in her life,' as a caregiver is needed, and she can't afford one, the outlet reported.

She cited a spike in his finances since the original agreement was hammered out, claiming Mane has done well professionally over the past few years since wrapping up in 2016 a three-year prison stint in a drug and gun conviction, citing his BET show, The Mane Event.

Mane asked the court to throw out her request, as he said his income had not substantially changed since their previous agreement eight years ago.

An insider told The Blast 'everyone is happy with the outcome' of the hearing.
