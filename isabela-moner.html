Isabela Moner Bio, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth, Wiki

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Isabela Merced or also known as Isabela Moner is an American actress and singer from Cleveland, Ohio. She is known for playing the role of CJ Marin on the Nickelodeon television series 100 Things to Do Before High School. She is currently signed with Republic Records for her music label.

Early life

At the time of her birth, she was named Isabela Yolanda Moner although later changed her last name recently in 2019 in honor of her late grandmother. Born on July 10, 2001, in Cleveland, Ohio, the United States to father Patrick Moner and mother Katherine Moner. She has two siblings, Jared and Giovanni Moner, Isabela is the middle child in the family. From a young age, Isabela was attracted to performing arts and singing. Her first play was when she was six years of age. For her schooling, Isabela was home-schooled at Ohio Virtual Academy, later she attended college at the age of 15.

Isabela Moner Career

Growing up, Isabela has always been fascinated with acting and singing. In 2008 and 2009 she took part in Youngstown Superstar Competition and Playhouse Square Rising Star, respectively, and won in both of them. She was cast in her first Broadway play Evita at just the age of 10 and the play received three Tony nominations. In 2014 she was seen playing Jenny in the sitcom Growing Up Fisher as a recurring role. She was also cast as the lead role of CJ Martin in Nickelodeon’s 100 Things to Do Before High School for which she even won Best Young Actor – Television at the 2016 Imagen Awards.

She worked with Broadway Records in September 2015 and released an album titled Stopping Time. In 2015 she appeared as Lori Collins in the television film, Spitting Adam. Next following year, Moner was cast as Sadie, one of the leading roles in Legends of the Hidden Temple, a Nickelodeon Original Movie. In the American comedy film, Middle School: The Worst Years of My Life Isabela played Jeanne Galleta in 2016. She was seen in Transformers: The Last Knight as Izabella, in 2018 Sicario: Day of the Soldado as Isabel Reyes, and in Instant Family as Lizzy.

In 2019 she played the lead role of Dora in the adventure film, Dora and the Lost City of Gold and was also cast as Julie in the Christmas romantic film, Let it Snow based on the same-titled novel. Merced released the better half of me, her debut EP in May 2020. Moner will be seen playing Rachel in the action thriller film Sweet Girl, the movie is post-production.
