Kanye West's wife Bianca wears 'stuffed toy' and $1k pumps in 'most obnoxious outfit yet'

February 2024 · 2 minute read

Kanye West and his wife, Bianca Censori, are no strangers to turning heads with their outfits. The couple’s looks throughout 2023 have included a variety of showstoppers including ‘Sims’ style fits and some accessorized with cushions.

Bianca is bringing out the unique accessories once again as she steps out wearing a taupe mini dress, and a furry hat, as well as a ‘stuffed toy’ bag. The two spent the summer vacationing in Europe and caused a stir with some of Bianca’s barely-there outfit choices. Now, it appears that she’s embracing the winter months as she enters a faux fur era.

Kanye West and Bianca’s ‘power couple’ outfit

Rapper Kanye and his wife are fast approaching their first anniversary this December.

As the two enjoy life as newlyweds, they’ve been spotted multiple times being super creative in the fashion field.

Both Ye and Bianca have been seen attending events this year including London Fashion Week and they’ve been catching fans’ attention all the while.

On November 29, the two attended a pop-up event in Dubai and once fan hailed them a “power couple.”

Posing for photos at the Amina Muaddi dinner, Kanye wore all black while Bianca put on a leggy display in a minidress, oversized faux fur hat, and completed the look with a brown deer bag.

Bianca’s bag is affordable

While Ye and Bianca are often spotted wearing expensive designer garments, the Head of Yeezy’s Architecture stepped out wearing a bag that costs around $180.

The brown deer bag comes from a brand called ‘Praying’ which states that it’s made from 100% polyester.

The website also writes that the accessory “features a beautiful zipper pouch to hold all of your beautiful belongings.”

Kanye’s wife opts for faux fur heels

After donning an oversized furry hat alongside her deer bag, Bianca later stepped out in Dubai once more in another eye-catching ensemble.

The 28-year-old was spotted wearing faux fur black heels which come from Balenciaga’s Spring 24 Collection, writes Instagram account @balencigirls.

In what some fans are saying is her “most obnoxious outfit yet,” Bianca opted for an all-black look with her fur-covered pointed pumps.

One fan commented on the look: “Bianca’s most obnoxious outfit yet I cannot.”

More joked that the footwear, worth $1,130, was giving: “Bigfoot’s wife.”
