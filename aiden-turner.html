Aiden Turner is a British Actor as well as a Model. Early Life and Education

Aiden Turner was born on April 2, 1977, in Welwyn Garden City, Hertfordshire, England. He is 46 years old and is under the astrological sign, Aries. He holds British nationality and his full name is Aiden John Turner.

His father’s name is Pat Turner and his mother’s name is Eileen Turner. He also has three siblings but their names are unknown at the moment. Other than this, information related to his other family members such as his cousins, grandparents, and relatives has not been available until now.

Regarding Turner’s educational background and qualifications, he attended local Catholic schools and they are St Joseph’s and St Hugh’s. He then went on to enrol at St Edmund’s College, Ware. And, when he was 16 to 18 years old, he went to culinary school and started mixing music as a DJ.

Career and Professional Life

Aiden Turner is popular for his role as ‘Aidan Devane’ on “All My Children”. He played in the series from June 6, 2002, until December 21, 2009. He first left the role in May 2004.

And, during that time the role was recast with actor Tom Archdeacon but Turner returned to the show a few months later and portrayed Devane until Turner left AMC on 21 December 2009. He was able to receive a nomination for an Emmy in the year 2003 for “Outstanding Younger Actor” in ‘All My Children’. He was able to appear in Geri Halliwell’s “Bag It Up” music video as well.

Likewise, he starred as a celebrity contestant on “Dancing with the Stars” for the tenth season. In the show, he became a partner with Edyta Sliwinska who competed in her tenth season.

The season premiered on March 22, 2010, and Turner was eliminated at the end of week 4 of Season 10. He also went on to guest-star on “Single Ladies, NCIS: Los Angeles” and “Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.”. Turner got the opportunity to appear in the Oprah Winfrey Network series “If Loving You Is Wrong” in the year 2014.

Moreover, other than acting, he is also a model and has found a successful career in voiceover as well as commercial work. He was able to appear in a CoverGirl commercial with Molly Sims.

He has modelled for Burberry, French Connection, and Polo Ralph Lauren, among others, and has appeared in commercials for Diet Coke, Head & Shoulders, Mercedes Benz, Dodge Dart, Clorox, and Chevrolet. Aiden was the voice of Jaguar for many years. He also went on to star in a horror movie, “Hunting Souls” as well as the movie “Mindcage”.

The pair also has a daughter together whose name is Stella Turner. Their relationship ended in the year 2010.
