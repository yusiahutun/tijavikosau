Get the Viral Jelly Sunglasses for $11

June 2024 · 4 minute read

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

If you have a sweet tooth, you’ll love Gentle Monster’s Gentle Jelly sunglasses collection. With candy-colored pastel frames and kawaii charms that look good enough to eat, the brand’s Gummy sunglasses have quickly gone viral on TikTok for their playful twist on sporty eyewear. Some styles—reminiscent of gummy worms—have pastel gradient colorways, while others are flocked in sugar. While the glasses are undeniably sweet, their price tag isn’t. Thankfully, there are Gentle Monster Jelly sunglasses dupes in tow.

The popularity of these glasses comes as no surprise—Gentle Monster is known for setting trends. The brand’s resume includes a previous collab with BLACKPINK’s Jennie, as well as a long list of A-list fans like Gigi Hadid, Billie Eilish, and Rihanna. And with its newest Gentle Jelly collection, the brand is making everyone feel like a kid in a candy store.

Gentle Monster Gummy PC12

The candy-inspired Gentle Jelly specs have taken TikTok by storm. And since then, the collection’s most popular styles have sold out on the Gentle Monster site. But even before they did, fans on TikTok were disappointed to see they cost upwards of $300.

If you’re obsessed with Gentle Monster’s gummy sunglasses but don’t want the Gentle Monster price tag, there are tons of similar sunglasses on the market for a fraction of the price. And thanks to the power of DIY, you can emulate the collection even more closely by gluing candy-inspired charms (not actual candy) on a pair of budget-friendly sunglasses, like TikToker KT did. 

Looking for Gentle Monster dupes of your own? I’ve found a bunch of great options—and the cheapest pair costs just $11. After all, you deserve some candy-colored sunglasses that’ll satisfy your sweet tooth without emptying your pockets.

  • SA106 Wraparound Curved Sunglasses

    SA106 Wraparound Curved Sunglasses

    Looking for baby pink sunglasses similar to Gentle Monster’s sold-out Yummy PC11 frames? These $11 Walmart sunglasses make a great dupe and come in the perfect shade of bubblegum pink. If you’re really ambitious, you can attempt to flock these sunglasses in faux sugar for a similar look—although they’re cute as is too.

  • Cotton Candy Sports Sunglasses

    Cotton Candy Sports Sunglasses

    Giant Vintage has another great dupe with its Cotton Candy Sports Sunglasses. Maybe I’m a bit late to the party with these, as only one of the six pastel shades is still in stock. Hopefully there’s a restock in the near future though, as these shades are so on par with the Gentle Monster ones.

  • JaneFrames Wraparound Sunglasses

    JaneFrames Wraparound Sunglasses

    These $13 sunglasses from Etsy are a great Gentle Monster dupe. They have that same wraparound sporty silhouette as the Gentle Jelly ones—not to mention, they come in the cutest candy purple shade.

  • Wild Fable Sport Matte Finish Sunglasses

    Wild Fable Sport Matte Finish Sunglasses

    For the neutral girlies, Target has a more subdued version of the Gummy sunglasses with that same sporty silhouette. Very similar to the gray Panna Cotta K5 (which is indeed still part of the Gentle Jelly collection), the sunglasses will help you channel your inner Gentle Monster it-girl in a more subtle fashion.

  • Slade Slim Plastic Shield Sunglasses

    Slade Slim Plastic Shield Sunglasses

    In the same vein as the Gentle Monster dupes from Target, Urban Outfitters also has some neutral options. The Slade Slim Plastic Shield Sunglasses come in both cream and gray. Although the silhouette is a bit more futuristic-looking than the Gentle Monster Gummy sunnies, they definitely offer a similar vibe.

  • Resin Candy Charms

    Resin Candy Charms

    If you’re feeling crafty, these Etsy candy charms are so similar to the ones seen on Gentle Monster’s Gummy MG1. For the perfect Gentle Jelly dupe, glue the charms on to one of the above styles. Etsy has no shortage of gummy charms, so feel free to mix and match to make your own unique Gentle Jelly model. Personally, I’d love to see some gummy bears thrown in for a truly mouthwatering look.
