Thank-you notes should be sent to thank someone for small favours such as a luncheon or treat. A thoughtful thank-you note makes you appear considerate and appreciative. Thank you note for luncheon

A luncheon is a formal lunch held in connection with a meeting or other special occasion. If someone invites you to a luncheon, you should express your sincere gratitude for the gesture by sending a thank you note or message. Here are sample thank you wordings for a luncheon that you can use to thank the person.

  • Hi, thank you for the luncheon. It was delicious, and it was fun getting to know everyone! Let's do it again sometime!
  • I just wanted to thank you for inviting me to that amazing luncheon. It was amazing, and the food was terrific! Thanks so much. Next time at our place.
  • Today's luncheon was tremendously pleasant, and I am very glad to have spent it with a superb person like you. Thank you so much for your act of generosity.
  • Thank you for that amazing luncheon! I had so much fun. I appreciate your kindness for allowing me to join you at the luncheon. Thank you!
