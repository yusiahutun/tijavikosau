Lisa of the pop music collective BLACKPINK celebrated her Crazy Horse cabaret performance in an Instagram post shared on Wednesday.

She wrote in the caption, 'Such an amazing experience at @crazyhorseparis_official Thank you everyone for making this happen. Call me whenever you need someone to fill a spot.'

BLACKPINK, a massively successful collective, made history as the first Korean music artists to headline a major UK music festival earlier this year.

The girl group - composed of Jisoo, Jennie, Rose, and Lisa - closed out the second weekend of BST Hyde Park in London to a sold-out crowd of 65,000 fans in July.

The band was made up of nine members at the time of its inception in 2016, before the final line-up was reduced to four.

According to People, a statement at the time explained: 'The meaning of BLACKPINK aims to contradict the common perception of the color pink. Pink is commonly used to portray prettiness, but BLACKPINK actually means to say that "Pretty isn't everything."'
