WWE star Nikkita Lyons has been branded a "goddess" by wrestling fans after she posted a series of snaps stripping down to bikini for some time at the beach.

The NXT star has been out of action since a taping in January of this year but made time to keep her followers up to date with her recovery. She recently posed for photos which led to fans praising her "goddess" status.

Lounging around on the beach for a series of Instagram photographs, Lyons stuck five photos up online yesterday (November 23) and received just shy of 100,000 likes.

It appears Lyons is attempting to enjoy her time out of action, taking to Instagram with the series of snaps and captioning the beach-set photos: "I love it here."

Out of action with a knee injury, the promising wrestler has taken time out to recover from a knock sustained in a match on NXT. A series of photos in a two piece bikini is her latest showcase of time spent outside of the ring.

Lyons has also recently appeared in a music video shoot and showcased life behind the scenes on her social media posts.

Known by her nickname The Lioness, Lyons revealed she had torn her medial collateral ligament back in January and was mysteriously taken out in a scripted event on the January 24 episode of NXT.

Writing at the time of the music video, which was reported by Ringside News, the star thanked her fans and shared a sneak peek as she attempted to bounce back.

She wrote: "I am thankful. Thank you all for being you. I'm infinitely grateful for your love and support you show everyday! Here's a lil sneak peak [sic] of what's to come along with the #PounceBack."
