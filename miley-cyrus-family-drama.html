Why Didnt Noah Attend Tishs Wedding?

April 2024 · 4 minute read

After some notable absences from Tish Cyrus’ wedding, people speculate that Miley Cyrus’ family drama is much bigger than we realize.

Billy Ray Cyrus and Tish Cyrus married in 1992. Together, they have 3 children, Miley (born 1992), Braison (born 1994), and Noah (born 1999). Billy Ray adopted Tish’s two children, Brandi (born 1987) and Trace (born 1989), who she had before they were together. Billy Ray also has a son Christopher Cody (born 1992) who was born to his ex-girlfriend and isn’t close to the family.

Billy Ray and Tish issued a statement to People on April 11, 2022 confirming their divorce, “We will always be family and look forward to a continued and loving shared experience as friends and parents. We have not come to this decision lightly or quickly but with so much going on in the world, we wanted to provide some clarity and closure, so we can remain focused on what is important.”

The couple had previously filed for divorce in 2010, but withdrew the case after amicable agreements. Tish also filed for divorce in 2013, but withdrew the petition five months later when the couple was going to couples therapy to amend their marriage. This past weekend on August 19, 2023, Tish Cyrus married Dominic Purcell in Malibu with a few of her children not present at the nuptials.

So what’s the further drama with Miley Cyrus’ family? Read more to find out.

  • Miley Cyrus Family Drama

    After the split, fans were quick to notice that Noah Cyrus blocked her mom on Instagram after it seemed like Tish couldn’t tag her in her old photos. Some fans panicked as it appeared that Noah unfollowed her mother. “they don’t follow each other anymore, the pics of Noah on Tish’s page the tags don’t show up anymore – which happens when someone blocks you,” one person explained on X, formerly known as Twitter.

    Months later, the beef settled with Noah finally following her mother again. Tish even posted an Instagram carousel of her youngest daughter in Paris. “@noahcyrus Paris suits you ❤️ Slaying❤️,” she captioned the post.

    Fast forward to last weekend, Noah and her brother Braison were not in attendance of Tish’s wedding to Dominic. Fans even went to say that she and her brother were shading them in several Instagram story posts. Noah was spotted with a shirt with her father Billy Ray Cyrus on in her Instagram stories. “my brother flew out to see me !” Noah posted a picture of her brother holding up a menu at a restaurant.

    “Noah Cyrus and Braison being absent at Tish’s wedding speaks volumes,” one X user posted. Another X user had the same sentiments. “noah posted about how braison flew from Nashville to LA “just to see her” this weekend and they had a sleepover and Noah was wearing a Billy Ray tshirt yesterday and skipped tish’s wedding speaks volumes. She and braison are mocking Tish”

    Meanwhile, the “Flowers” singer served as maid of honor at her mother’s wedding, with Brandi and Trace in attendance. On Trace’s Instagram story, he posted a throwback photo of him, Tish, Miley, and Brandi when they were younger. Tish later reposted Trace’s story onto her story.

  • Miley Cyrus Reaction To Parents' Divorce

    At the time, a close insider of the Cyruses told HollywoodLife on April 12, 2022, that the family is supportive of Billy Ray and Tish’s divorce, “Miley and her siblings have been expecting this for over a year now, as their parents have not been living together for several years and they have not been happy together for as long as anyone can remember,” the insider said. 

    “When Miley shot to fame, Tish and Billy’s marriage was always more of a business relationship than an actual marriage,” the source continued. “The romance and passion were gone long ago, and they seemingly stayed together for the sake of their children. But they do not need to raise any of them anymore,” the source said.

    Miley is no stranger to divorces. A year after their wedding, the Plastic Hearts singer finalized her divorce from Liam Hemsworth in January 2020 citing “too much conflict” in their marriage. The couple married in 2018, but were romantically linked since starring in The Last Song in 2009. Miley revealed in a December 2, 2019, The Howard Stern Show episode that she “did love him very, very, very much and still do, always will.”

    “Miley, more than any of her siblings, knows what it is like to be stuck in a marriage where neither of you is happy,” HollywoodLife’s insider explained. “She knows what it is like to love someone with all your heart, but no longer be in love with them.”
