Margot Robbie stripping off for X-rated scene in The Wolf Of Wall Street nearly cost her huge role

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Margot Robbie, known for her sex symbol status, nearly saw her Hollywood career go down the drain due to her risque nude scenes in films like The Wolf Of Wall Street.

The 33 year old actress starred alongside Leonardo DiCaprio in the steamy crime film back in 2013, which quickly proved to be her breakout role. In one unforgettable scene, Margot, playing Naomi Lapaglia, bared it all to seduce Leonardo's character, Jordan Belfort.

Fans worldwide were smitten with the Aussie actress' performance, catapulting her to stardom. However, her bold nude scene almost cost her a role in Mary Queen of Scots.
