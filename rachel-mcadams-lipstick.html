Rachel McAdams at the 'Southpaw' Premiere

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Is anyone cooler than Rachel McAdams? We don’t think so. She’s the best thing about this season of True Detective and there’s already serious buzz around her role in Southpaw. Plus, she was in Mean Girls, perhaps the most important film of the last 15 years. And her red carpet style is always elegant and stylish; a perfect mixture of Old Hollywood and effortless beauty.

At the New York premiere of Southpaw, we were stunned by her amazing berry lipstick. We generally think of berry lips as a Fall thing, but when the color is kept sheer and glossy, it’s perfect for humid summer days.

The work of celebrity makeup artist Daniel Martin, McAdams’ lip color is Dior Addict Lipstick in So Dior ($33), a brand new shade coming out soon. Dior makes some of our favorite lip products, but the Addict line of lipsticks are easily our favorites. Sheer, comfortable and hydrating, they aren’t sticky and last for hours. The colors are buildable and easy to apply, so you can easily swipe on a coat as you run out the door, or apply a couple of layers for more impact. Plus, the iridescent case? BEYOND cute.

So Dior is a rich berry shade that’s glossy rather than sparkly, which makes it perfect to wear at the office or a hot date. And because it’s so sheer and luscious-looking, not to mention long-wearing, it’s the ideal lipstick for this intensely hot weather we’re having.

“That’s how you do chic when you’re up against the elements,” says Martin, and we could not agree more.
