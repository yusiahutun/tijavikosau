Bamburi Cement contacts and branches

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Bamburi Cement is a Kenyan company that provides quality and innovative solutions for the construction industry. The company is a subsidiary of LafargeHolcim, the leading cement manufacturing, and marketing company in Eastern Africa. The construction solutions produced by Bamburi Cement include cement, blocks and ready-mix concrete. The company’s operations are majorly based in the Bamburi suburb of Mombasa. However, Bamburi Cement headquarters is in Nairobi. This post will provide a list of Bamburi Cement contacts and branches.

In Kenya, it is almost impossible for one to think about construction without the word Bamburi coming into mind. The company branches fall into Bamburi Special Products, Lafarge Eco Systems, and Bamburi Cement Grinding Plant. Services offered by Bamburi Cement

Bamburi is Kenya’s largest supplier of concrete paving blocks. Apart from the manufacture of various products such as paving blocks, Bamburi Cement also offers several services that include:

  • Free site inspection and advice
  • Delivery of products
  • Paving designs
  • Full laying service
  • Pumping facilities
  • Strict quality control of constituent materials and end products
  • Technical support
