16 and Pregnant star Sean Garinger dies in tragic accident after ground suddenly gave way

May 2024 · 2 minute read

16 and Pregnant star Sean Garinger, known as Selena Gutierrez’s ex, has been confirmed dead aged 20 following a horrific ATV accident. It happened when the father moved an ATV into a new parking spot.

Sean Garinger starred on 16 and Pregnant with his then-girlfriend Selena Gutierrez. They welcomed two children on the Teen Mom spin-off together – daughters Dareli in October 2020 and Esmi in June 2022.

Sean Garinger on 16 and Pregnant dies

Sean Garinger from 16 and Pregnant has been confirmed dead. The father of two died on February 28 at his home in Boone, who was with his mom, Mary, when the incident happened.

An ATV “flipped and crushed his skull,” Mary told The US Sun. “I was with him when it happened. He was just moving [the ATV] from one parking spot to the next for me, so I could back into park.”

“He pulled [in] front of me to park the ATV and the ground gave way from all the rain and mud,” she added. Paying tribute, Mary said, Sean brightened my world in a way no one else could come close to.”

His mom realised he ‘wasn’t alive’

Sean’s mom, 40, who had just gotten home from the car dealership with him, told The US Sun, “I ran to neighbors trying to get someone to help me get the ATV off of him. No one answered. I ran back to him.”

“By that time, I realized he wasn’t alive anymore. I just laid next to him until the ambulance showed up,” she added. The plan was to buy a car so Sean could drive to see his girls in Colorado where Selena lives.

However, they “never made it back to the dealership,” his mom added. She said, “There was a huge part of my heart that died with my son on Wednesday. He was my only son, my rock, my strength.”

Selena and Sean on 16 and Pregnant

Selena and Sean welcomed two daughters together on 16 and Pregnant season 6B. The former couple welcomed a daughter Dareli in October 2020 and another daughter Esmi in June 2022.

They nicknamed their second daughter Esmi “squishy,” which is what Sean’s nickname was growing up. But the duo split after having two children, including a fight that led to Sean getting arrested.

At the time of Sean’s death, it is believed both he and Selena were dating other people. While Selena primarily looked after their daughters, Sean often went to Colorado to visit them.
