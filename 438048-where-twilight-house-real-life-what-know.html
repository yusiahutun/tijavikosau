Where is Bella's house from Twilight?

Fans of the vampire show have also been keen to discover where Bella Swan's residence is. This house is famous because Bella and Edward's first kiss happens here. Other Twilight houses

Unknown to many viewers, the Twilight sequels New Moon and Eclipse were filmed in a different house. The house is located in West Vancouver, British Properties, 118 Stevens Drive and was designed by Arthur Erickson.

The mansion was renovated in 2001 and looked almost like the Oregon one but had notable differences like the driveway. Once the filming was complete, the house was listed for approximately $3.2 million.
