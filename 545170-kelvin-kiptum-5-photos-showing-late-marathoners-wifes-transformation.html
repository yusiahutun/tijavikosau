Kelvin Kiptum: 5 Photos Showing Late Marathoner's Wife's Transformation

September 2024 · 3 minute read
  • Kelvin Kiptum's wife, Asenath Rotich, represented his family at the Rotterdam Marathon, which was held last weekend in the Netherlands
  • The mother of two stepped out both in official, casual and sporty wear while attending different events, and fans noticed her transformation, admitting she was glowing
  • Asenath and Kiptum were together for 10 years and were to tie the knot in a white wedding when the world record holder died

Asenath Rotich, the wife of late marathoner Kelvin Kiptum, flew out of the country for the first time after his burial.

On April 12, a day before the Rotterdam Marathon, Kiptum's wife attended a sports event in the Netherlands before the competition, which was slated for Sunday, April 14.

Asenath wore a blue trouser suit, a white top, sandals and cute earrings.

The mother of two, while in the Netherlands, stepped up make-up-free and a simple hairstyle.

They say fashion is something you're comfortable in, and Kiptum's wife's latest fashion has got tongues wagging.

4. Asenath Rotich's sporty look
