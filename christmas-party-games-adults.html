30 Best Adult Christmas Games for Adult Parties 2023

June 2024 · 4 minute read
1

Christmas Movie Bingo

Becky Stayner

Pop some popcorn, bake up some brownies, and make some hot chocolate for a Christmas movie night! Print off some copies of our Christmas Movie Bingo game to get the whole family involved in the fun. Or queue up your favorite Hallmark Christmas movie and try your hand at Hallmark Christmas Movie Bingo!

2

Hallmark Christmas Movie Drinking Game

Design: Emily Schiff-Slater

Here's a great party game for your next movie night—a Hallmark Christmas movie, to be exact. Talk about getting into the Christmas spirit!

Get the tutorial.

3

Christmas Movies Emoji Game

Play Party Plan

Can you guess these classic Christmas movies just from their emoji description? Put your skills to the test with this clever game.

Get the tutorial at Play Party Plan.

4

Christmas Alphabet Drinking Game

Betsie Van der Meer//Getty Images

Go through the alphabet in a circle, as each person says a Christmas related name, item, song, and so on, for the letter they have. If you hesitate or draw a blank, you have to drink!

5

Christmas Card Balderdash

Poh Kim Yeoh / EyeEm//Getty Images

See which party-goer could be the next Hallmark card writer! This game challenges guests to write the message they think is on the inside of a card. Then everyone guesses which greeting they think was the real one written on the card.

Get the tutorial at Play Party Plan.

6

Gift Wrap Game

Nicolas Balcazar / EyeEm//Getty Images

Challenge your party guests to see who can wrap a present the fastest. The catch? They must work in pairs and each person can only use one hand!

Get the tutorial at Party Game Ideas.

Everyone's heard of this classic party game! However, this one is not only Christmas-inspired, but it's also adults-only too.

10

'Never Have I Ever' Christmas Edition

Caiaimage/Robert Daly//Getty Images

Have you ever re-gifted something? If so, you might just lose this game. The punishment? Every time you've done something, you have to take a drink of your holiday cocktail.

Get the tutorial at Tea With Gi.

11

The Santa Hat Game

the_burtons//Getty Images

At the beginning of the party, each person is given a Santa hat and a piece of paper. However, only one person is "it" and whenever they take their hat off, everyone else must follow suit. The last person with their hat on loses.

12

Snow Sled Race

praetorianphoto//Getty Images

If your lucky and have plenty of snow falling in your neck of the woods, gather the gang for a fun sled race! Put on your cozy boots and heavy coats to line up at the top of a nearby hill. Space each team apart at a safe distance and appoint a ref to count off the start. The first team at the bottom of the hill wins this festive game!

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

13

Truth or Dare

AleksandarGeorgiev//Getty Images

You know the rules. And they're basically the same—just add Santa hats—and decidedly holiday dares (think making the next beer run in a Santa suit or sit on everyone's lap and tell them what you want for Christmas!).

14

Candy Canes

Wirestock//Getty Images

If you've ever played the Spoons card game, we think you'll love this version even more. The reason? Instead of cutlery, you use candy canes!

15

Two Truths and a Lie

Getty Images

Pick three "facts" (think anything from your favorite Christmas memories to the worst gifts you've ever been given), and see how many people can pick the lie from three statements.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

16

Jingle Bell Toss

Playground Park Bench

What could be more entertaining than a beer pong-cornhole hybrid? Hot-glue cups to a board and toss bells into them to score.

Get the tutorial at Playground Park Bench.

17

Christmas Limbo

Getty Images

How low can you go? Use a Christmas scarf or candy cane prop to make this party game more festive, and for even more of a challenge, tuck a balloon in your shirts for "Santa" bellies.

18

How Many Ornaments?

Getty Images

At the beginning of the night, have each guest guess the number of ornaments on the tree for a clever take on the candies-in-a-jar game. Record names and numbers on pieces of paper, and award the person with the closest guess a prize (a special ornament, perhaps?).

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

19

Name That Christmas Carol

skynesher//Getty Images

Compile a list of lyrics (can be obvious or lesser-known) from Christmas songs and see how many each team can guess in one minute. Or, instead of lyrics, hum a portion of the tune for your team to guess. Warning: Things might get loud.

20

Elf on the Shelf Game for Adults

Mommy Savers

After the kids have gone to sleep, break out ol' Elf on the Shelf and have an adults-only contest to see who can come up with the funniest placement.

Get the details at Mommy Savers.
